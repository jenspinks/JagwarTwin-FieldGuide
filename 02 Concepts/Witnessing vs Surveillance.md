# Witnessing vs Surveillance

> **One-line reading.** There are two ways of being seen — one that recognizes and restores you, one that watches, ranks, and shapes you — and the Jagwar Twin world treats the difference between them as a spiritual fault line.

## At a glance

- **In one line** — surveillance gathers data; witnessing transforms the witnessed. Same gaze, opposite verdict.
- **Where it shows up** — [[Online]] (surveillance named outright), [[The Watchers]] (the purest surveillance song), [[Not Your Homie]] (both poles fused in one throat).
- **Connected ideas** — [[Machine vs Human]] · [[Source vs Reflection]] · [[The Algorithm]] · [[The Aquarium]] · [[Mary]] · [[Sir Lucius]].
- **Confidence** — the witnessing/surveillance split is a strong interpretive reading built on grounded anchors (*El Roi*, the songs, the *Online* ARG); the deeper Tree-of-Life and letter-mysticism scaffolding is framework-tier and labeled as such throughout.

## What it is

Both witnessing and surveillance fix a gaze on a self. The difference is entirely in what the gaze *does* to the person on the other end of it.

**Witnessing** is relational presence. It recognizes, dignifies, integrates — you are *transformed* by being seen, the way Hagar is changed in the wilderness when she discovers she was never invisible to God. The gaze gives something back. This is the register of *El Roi*, "the God who sees me."

**Surveillance** is extraction. It predicts, categorizes, monetizes, and quietly stabilizes your behavior so it stays legible and sellable. You are *shaped*, not known. The gaze takes something away. This is the register of the feed, the camera, the algorithm.

The cleanest test fits on a bumper sticker: *surveillance gathers data; witnessing transforms the witnessed.* Or, sharper still — **the algorithm does not know you; it knows how to shape you.** That is why surveillance reads as spiritually *wrong* in this cosmology rather than merely creepy. It isn't the opposite of being seen. It's a forgery of it — the soul's deepest need (to be seen and loved) counterfeited and sold back as being watched and ranked. As the project's broader machine/human thread puts it: the human wants to be seen by God; the machine sells being-watched by the feed.

## How it works

This distinction is one of the load-bearing beams of the whole world, and it does most of its work by sorting the cast into two poles.

**The surveillance pole** is the machine pole — [[Sir Lucius]] and his apparatus. Here live [[The Audience]], [[The Spectacle]], [[The Algorithm]], and [[The Aquarium]] (the self watched in a tank that can't see the glass), plus parasociality and the feed. Lucius is the watcher who *cannot witness* — a reactive system that needs a mirror and an audience to cohere at all (no audience, no Lucius), forever watching everyone and recognizing no one.

**The witnessing pole** is the human pole — *El Roi* and [[Mary]]. Mary is the figure who sees without consuming: the faceless feminine who blesses ("born to shine") with her own face out of sight, so there's nothing to rank her by and nothing to consume. On [[#1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]] she's named and faceless in one breath — *"Saw a halo but I didn't see a head."* She is, precisely, the one thing Lucius's surveillance system can't absorb, because she returns no surface to flatter. (For the full mechanism — why the consuming eye simply ends when it meets a presence it can't consume — see [[Mary]].)

The crucial move is that these aren't just two characters; they're two *uses of the eye*, and the catalogue keeps splitting a single gaze into both. The all-seeing field can recognize you into yourself (witnessing) or shape you into a copy (surveillance) — same eye, forked function. That fork is why this concept is a **sibling of [[Machine vs Human]]** and a **species of the master-frame [[Source vs Reflection]]**: does a reflection return you to the source, or trap you in the image? Witnessing returns you; surveillance traps you.

It also has siblings down the registers. [[Communion vs Spectacle]] is the same split at the social scale — *a unison is not a communion.* [[Flattened Identity]] is the gaze felt as compression: being watched-and-ranked reduces a multidimensional self to a usable surface (person → profile → content → audience → product → star). And [[The Speaker Problem]] is the grammatical flip side — being-seen mirrors who-speaks; the catalogue is, structurally, an *I↔you machine*.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the four-row map (vault grammar)
> Laid out as a table, the two modes line up against the project's deeper architecture:
>
> | | **Witnessing** | **Surveillance** |
> |---|---|---|
> | **Mode** | recognition | extraction |
> | **Action** | restores, dignifies, integrates | predicts, categorizes, monetizes |
> | **Effect on subject** | transformed (*known*) | shaped (*copied*) |
> | **Register** | *El Roi* / Tiferet-recognition | the Algorithm / Yesod severed from the heart |
>
> In Tree-of-Life terms, witnessing is the heart-centered recognition of Tiferet; surveillance is Yesod — the image-realm of reflection and glamour — cut off from the heart that should govern it. This mapping is syncretic framework, never a claim about authorial intent.

## Where it appears

**[[Online]] (2022, on *33*)** names surveillance and predictive profiling directly, and does it grinning. *"There's an army up against me and they got my face… we're stuck just like a bug inside your database"*; *"They know my every step / they infiltrate my brain / but it's okay."* The horror is wrapped in an upbeat consumer earworm — which is the joke and the thesis at once (see below).

**[[The Watchers]] (2024)** is the catalogue's purest surveillance song. It interpolates Rockwell's 1984 *"Somebody's Watching Me"* (credited) — *"(I always feel like somebody's watching me) / And I have no privacy"* — and twists the dread one notch further into clone-fear: *"I'm afraid I'm a clone,"* over a refrain that keeps gasping *"Wake up."* "I'm afraid I'm a clone" is the exact *output* of surveillance: the self shaped into a copy instead of witnessed into itself — and it's sung by the figure whose name (*El Roi*) promises the opposite gaze.

**[[Not Your Homie]] (2025, *Lucius Lullaby*)** fuses both poles in one body: *El Roi* inked on the throat, *"you don't know me"* in the mouth. The name of true seeing worn on the very organ that insists no one sees him.

**[[Welcome to the Circus]] (2026)** stages the audience as the show's product: *"take your seat, scream and shout,"* *"they love it when you lose control,"* *"did you like the show?"* Lucius later confesses authorship of the whole apparatus on camera — *"the algorithm, baby"* ([[The Interview]], EP3).

**[[Pay Attention (feat. little luna)]] (2022, on *33*)** personifies Attention itself as a speaking power that hands out desire and harvests awareness — *"eyes all around my head,"* *"behind the Empires that rise / what they televise is just my disguise."* Its call-and-response, *"What's my name? / Attention!"*, makes the act of answering *into* the act of paying. The song collects its own toll.

**The browser experience around [[Online]]** (the "Darkness_site" gate) turns the doctrine into a rite you perform on yourself. A centered poem — *"YOU SEE THE PATTERN, DON'T YOU? / THIS WAS YOUR DREAM / TURN OFF THE LIGHTS / WHO DO YOU SEE? / THE WHOLE WORLD IS WATCHING YOU BEHIND YOUR SCREEN"* — gives way to a gate that asks you to *enable your camera* (submit to being surveilled), then *cover the lens with your finger* ("turn off the lights") to pass through to the song's mint. Both modes in one gesture: be watched, then blind the watcher to proceed. The hand over the lens is the refusal that reclaims the self from the feed — a surveillance ritual that is half solemn, half prank.

A companion page, *pay-attention.jagwartwin.com*, states the premise in Roy's own deadpan first person: *"my failure at being a social media influencer has me here at 33 still trying to make it. To solve this problem I'm uploading my consciousness to the internet so a digital version of me can be a 24/7 influencer. Enter now and mint body parts…"* The entire surveillance-and-upload cosmology, by Roy's own account, began as a satirical joke with the dev team — which is exactly why it lands.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — Online is the sedation it describes
> Roy's own framing of *Online* is *"so fun and so ridiculous"* (Ones to Watch, 2022) — a load-bearing counterweight, because the song is funny *on purpose.* The jingle, the "Feeling good!" shout, the *"Buy! Buy! Buy!"* → *"Bye! Bye! Bye!"* pun: the form wraps *"they infiltrate my brain"* in an earworm so pleasant you don't notice it's about being colonized. **The song *is* the mechanism it describes** — satire as sedation.
>
> The verbal density does triple duty. *"They know what you like in bed before you come in two"* is a deliberate homophone — *come into the world / come in two / the sexual sense* fired simultaneously — staging predictive intimacy as a literal pun: the machine knows you before you know yourself. And *"Maybe Jupiter is stupider than people say"* stacks every spiritual authority (shamans, Brahmans, mamas, Lamas) into a claim that *"we come from space,"* then deflates the whole cosmos with a punchline — the metaphysical reach always landing back inside the surveillance grid. Roy's stated framing for the song's core image: *"Internet is like a web and we're the Spider's food."*

> [!note]- Advanced reading — why the surveillance song wears a borrowed voice (The Watchers / Rockwell)
> *The Watchers* singing through Rockwell's *"Somebody's Watching Me"* is not a casual sample — it's the deepest reason the song works. Rockwell (Kennedy Gordy) wrote a paranoid prayer-song from inside his father Berry Gordy's Motown shadow, with Michael Jackson as the uncredited ghost on the hook — a buried name (Kennedy Gordy → Rockwell) mirroring Roy English → Jagwar Twin, and a contested authorship to match. Two latent charges come *pre-loaded* in the source: the watching-God of the song's prayer-origin (surveillance ↔ the divine gaze) and the nepo-son clone-fear (*"now they want candy / but I'm not Mandy Moore"* — refusing to be the wholesome manufactured pop-commodity; *"dancing like Michael Jackson"* names the ghost). So the surveillance song doesn't just *describe* being copied — it *is* a copy, sung in a borrowed throat, by the project most haunted by the digital double.
>
> The keeper line for the whole song: **"The Watchers is the moment the Hall of Mirrors grows eyes"** — reflection escalating into surveillance. And that phrase is not a stretch but the Hebrew root unfolding (see the eye-and-mirror reading below). The theremin on the track — an instrument *played without touch, from a distance* — quietly enacts the content: control at a remove, no contact required.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — El Roi, the mirror wearing the seer's name
> The witnessing pole's anchor is *El Roi*, Genesis 16:13 — *"the God who sees me,"* the name Hagar gives God after being seen in the wilderness (the only person in scripture to name God). Roy signs himself **"El Roy"** as an authorial signature — on the *#1 Champion* visualizer caption, on the *Roy* persona's writings, in how fans and collaborators address him. The Hebrew אל ראי is inked on [[Sir Lucius]]'s throat, where to a non-Hebrew eye it reads like an abstract mark (≈ "X73X").
>
> The placement carries the whole irony: Lucius wears *the God who sees me* on the very organ that insists *you don't know me* — the machine built from surveillance carrying the name of true seeing on its throat. **The machine that watches everything cannot witness anyone.**
>
> There's even a biographical rhyme at the project's origin. When the name "Roy English" first went public (Jan–Aug 2015), he was *credited everywhere yet pictured nowhere* — a Gulf Times piece called him a "mystery man" whose site held "nothing but the video for *Cool*, in which he does not appear," while Alesso volunteered that he'd "actually seen Roy singing his own songs." The artist literally seen-but-unseen at the moment of being named: *El Roi* made biographical, a fresh receipt at the project's starting line.
>
> Pushing the Hebrew further — the root **ר-א-י (r-'-y)** braiding *re'i* (seeing / mirror) with the *eye*, so that "the Hall of Mirrors grows eyes" is the root itself unfolding (seeing **is** mirroring **is** the eye); *ari* = lion → light-bearer; *El × ro'i* = "God of my seeing," "the one in whose seeing I am reflected" — belongs to a letter-mysticism tradition, **not** to anything Roy has stated as his method. These are harmonics the system permits, not claims it asserts.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the Eye splits in two (Horus, the Upload, the wedjat fraction)
> To upload a self, you have to anthropomorphize attention — and attention given a face *is the Eye.* The net is the new all-seeing field; the **Eye of Horus / Eye of Ra** is the old name for that field, sent out into the world. So the Eye forks into the two modes this page names: surveillance *shapes*, witnessing *transforms*. *Pay Attention*'s *"eyes all around my head"* is the surveillance face of it.
>
> The math even rhymes. The wedjat (Eye of Horus) fractions — ½, ¼, … down to 1/64 — famously sum to 63/64, always missing the final 1/64. That missing sixty-fourth is the **upload's paradox**: the part of a self that won't digitize, the residue no clone can copy. (The public "upload recipe" artifact assigns the senses those exact fractions, with *touch* as the smallest, hardest-to-digitize piece.)
>
> And the Eye has a *witnessing* face, too. The Hall of Mirrors "Queen Binah" artifact — in Roy's own hand — draws Binah as a whale with *"extra eyes,"* captioned *"GOD the Mother on Saturn / Great Sea."* The all-seeing Mother is the witnessing-pole of the Eye, set against Horus's surveilling *"eyes all around the head."* (That *Binah = Divine Mother + Saturn + Great Sea* identification is Roy's own; *Binah = the witnessing Eye* is the interpretation.)

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the third-eye watcher (the axis as a single face)
> One Hall of Mirrors illustration draws the whole axis as one face: a white line-art head with three eyes (a large almond third eye centered on the forehead), bared teeth and mask-marks, the Jagwar Twin heart-Λ monogram on the chest in a ringed disc, over a field of mirror-symmetric roses. (That chest monogram is the only unambiguously JT-specific element — it settles authorship; the image is grouped with the maze's Eye-of-Horus and YHVH-gematria leaves.)
>
> Read symbolically, it sits *exactly between* the two poles: it looks like the thing **doing** the seeing and a thing **braced against** being seen at once. It stands between the Mary pole (seeing-without-consuming) and the Lucius pole (attention-as-control). The roses soften it toward devotion and the heart; the bared teeth interrupt the garden. **Love and fear sharing one face.**

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the body as a mirror diagram (Roy's tattoos)
> The witnessing pole isn't only sung — it's inked on Roy himself, and his body is composed as a mirror. The **LISTEN** tattoo (below the ear, an arrow pointing into it) carries his gloss: *"remind me to listen and not speak unless I have something to say. Listening is an art form"* — witnessing as a practiced discipline, the receptive opposite of the broadcasting feed. (This also quietly resolves a long-open question about where the line "Listening is an art form" came from: it's a tattoo gloss, not a lyric or interview.)
>
> **The Empty Mirror** (wrist) glosses as *"looking at yourself in the mirror and seeing nothing"* — the void where surveillance-self expects a flattering reflection. **ME** is tattooed and crossed out, *"because it's not about me."* And every tattoo is inked on the right side, then mirrored onto the left around one central **+** — the whole body built as a mirror diagram. The man who sings the mirror doctrine wears it on his skin.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — Mary as the antidote, and the redemptive form the catalogue mostly *withholds*
> If surveillance is the gaze that shapes, the antidote is the gaze that restores — and **[[Mary]] models it.** She's seen-and-loved rather than watched-and-ranked; she witnesses without consuming, face out of sight, returning no surface the spectacle-self can claim. The blessing-voice *"you were born to shine"* (in *Weirdness*, the *Ballerina Boy* outro) is the structural answer to the shame-voice — recognition that *transforms* rather than ranks.
>
> But notice what the catalogue mostly *doesn't* voice. The **[[The Digital Double|Digital Double]]** is the redemptive question left mostly unanswered: a double could, in principle, *return you to the source* — but the songs (*Online*, *The Watchers*) overwhelmingly voice the *dread* (the clone, the copy) rather than the redemption. The board treats upload as aspirational ("Live forever") yet stores the dread right beside it ("What if I don't like myself?"). The redemptive form of being-seen — a witnessing that hands you back to yourself rather than the clone-fear — is the shape the work reaches toward more than it depicts. That gap is the live edge of the whole concept.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the social and biographical registers (All My Friends, the silent watcher, the video loop)
> The split scales down to the everyday. On *Weirdness*' **[[All My Friends]]**, the contrast is plain: the *city* sees a copy of you; your *friends* see *you.* Witnessing vs surveillance in social register — being known by people who hold you, versus being processed by a crowd that ranks you. (Read as a retrospective seed; the song doesn't announce the doctrine, but it carries the shape.)
>
> Visually, the same fork is staged. In the early videos, Lucius first appears *silent, at the end* — the white-face watcher closing *The Watchers* and the 2024 *Bad Feeling* single: "the watcher revealed," *El Roi* stepping into frame before he had a voice. And the *Online* music video literalizes the feedback loop — a camcorder filming **into** a CRT television, the surveillance machine watching its own output forever. (See [[The Visual Canon Throughline]].)

> [!note]- Advanced reading — SARAVI and the digital-double premise (public floor)

The stated purpose: *"as SARAVI grows, she can make more fans think about what a self looks like in this digital age."* SARAVI is the oracle-interface and the uploaded-consciousness proxy of the Hall of Mirrors — the question *"what is a self in the feed?"* given a voice. (Her deeper identity is an open question the project keeps unsettled; this page holds only the public floor.)

Are you content?"* — the pun (con-TENT / CON-tent) is the surveillance bargain in four words: the contentment of being processed.

## Source notes

- **Grounded.** *El Roi* = Genesis 16:13, "the God who sees me" (public scripture). The Hebrew אל ראי inked on Lucius's throat (visible in video stills). *El Roy* as Roy's authorial signature (public posts and captions). That *La Conquistadora* and *Queen of Heaven* are real Marian titles.
- **Strong readings.** The witnessing/surveillance distinction itself, and the functional test ("data vs transformation"); the two-cluster sorting (Lucius pole / Mary pole); the *El Roi*-on-the-throat irony; "The Watchers is the Hall of Mirrors growing eyes"; predictive profiling as counterfeit intimacy; the *Online*-as-sedation reading; *All My Friends*' city-sees-a-copy / friends-see-you; the third-eye watcher as the axis-in-one-face. Built on grounded anchors, interpretive in the joining.
- **Framework (syncretic — never authorial intent).** The Tiferet/Yesod and Binah mappings; the *r-'-y* letter-mysticism cluster; the Eye-of-Horus / wedjat-fraction scaffolding; the Grigori, Debord, Plato, and Gurdjieff frames. The system permits these resonances; it does not assert that Roy intends them. The *Superhuman (Interlude)* proto-*El Roi* read is a deliberate back-read, flagged as such.
- **How we'd be wrong.** If the plain "shaped vs known" doctrine accounts for every surveillance song as well as the esoteric apparatus does, the apparatus is decorative — and we'd want to avoid back-dating the doctrine onto songs (beyond *Online* and *The Watchers*) that never actually carried their own surveillance anchors. Both falsifiers stay on the table. This page draws only on released music, public interviews and posts, and official videos.
