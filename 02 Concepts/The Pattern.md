# The Pattern

> **One-line reading.** Roy's own name for the single law that repeats at every scale — "as above, so below," the same formula from the solar system down to the atom, encoded in myth, film, music, biology, and number, until reality reads as one fractal correspondence in which the self can be found.

## At a glance

- **In one line** — the idea that there is *one* structure, and it shows up everywhere — and the working name Roy gives it.
- **Where it shows up** — [[Another Way II Heaven]] (the creation myth as a released song) · [[Down to You]] (the 2021 "AS ABOVE SO BELOW" end-card) · [[Soul Is A Star]] (the Sun/Son draft).
- **Connected ideas** — [[Hermeticism - As Above So Below]] · [[Source vs Reflection]] · [[Duality]] · [[The Loop and the Circle]] · [[The Upload]] · [[Inherited Substrate]].
- **Confidence** — grounded as Roy's *own* term and frame (it's literally written in his hand); the per-song claims built on top of it are interpretation, and flagged as such throughout.

## What it is

Most of the framework words in this guide are ones *we* coined to describe the work. The Pattern isn't. It's Roy's. He wrote it down — on a sprawling personal spreadsheet board, in the cell about Hermetic correspondence — and the line reads, flatly, *"The Pattern is present on all levels."*

The thing it names is old and simple to say and bottomless to actually hold: there is one structure, and it recurs at every scale. *"As above, so below."* The same formula from the solar system to the atom — *"Adam / Hurricane / Galaxy / Fibonacci,"* as the board lists them, all running the one design. Spiral down into the small and you find the shape that organizes the large; zoom out and the galaxy is built like the seed. If that's true, then myth, film, music, biology, and number aren't separate subjects — they're the same sentence written in different alphabets. And — this is the part that makes it matter to the songs rather than just to a physics lecture — *you* are in the correspondence too. The self can be read off the pattern, because the self is one more instance of it.

That's the whole idea, and it's worth saying plainly before the apparatus arrives: the Pattern is the conviction that everything rhymes, and that the rhyme is legible.

## How it works

Here is the guardrail that has to come first, because everything downstream leans on it. Roy *holds* a Pattern-framework — that's grounded; we can read it in his own handwriting. But "Roy believes reality is one fractal correspondence" and "this song encodes the Pattern" are two very different claims, and only the first one is free. The second has to be earned song by song.

The beautiful thing is that Roy installs that guardrail himself. In the same breath as building the whole correspondence cosmology, he shrugs it off: *"I would map a crow onto Mercury, but that's just personal. I don't recommend you do it."* The author runs the mapping *and* disclaims it as personal and non-prescriptive — the anti-overfit warning, in his own voice. So the rule for this whole page, and frankly the safest rule for the whole guide, is: **license the lens, not a master key.** The Pattern is a substrate Roy works *from*, not a decoder ring that unlocks everything. It's the same risk we flag for [[The Labors of Hercules]] — a real frame Roy engages, which becomes a trap the moment you start back-fitting it onto songs that don't carry it on their own.

What the Pattern *does* across the work, then, is quieter than "explains every lyric." It explains why the world *coheres*. Because the Pattern braids together a particular set of traditions — Hermeticism, Kabbalah, Neoplatonism, Jung, Bailey, Russell — and because those traditions share a perennialist root, the project's other concepts naturally *rhyme* with it: [[Source vs Reflection]] echoes "as above, so below"; [[Duality]] echoes the principle of polarity; the [[The Loop and the Circle|Circle]] echoes the cosmic cycle. The honest read of that rhyme is that it's a property of the shared root — not, by itself, proof that Roy deliberately cross-encoded one into the other. The right move is to name the contributing thinkers in passing, let the concept be the thing, and not build the catalogue into a secret cipher. The Pattern is the reason the world feels like one world; it isn't a promise that every door has the same key.

And there's one place the frame turns from cosmology into instruction — the practical wing, where "everything corresponds" becomes "so here's what to *do* about it." On the board that wing is a plan to upload his own consciousness to the internet, to "live forever" by entering something that outlasts him. That material got its own home — see [[The Upload]] — because it's a distinct move (the Pattern as a doctrine of persistence) and it deserved room. The Pattern keeps the law; The Upload keeps the application.

## Where it appears

The primary source is a single document, and it's worth being concrete about what it is, because its very existence is half the comedy and half the evidence. It's a personal spreadsheet titled **"I'm 33 this year,"** on a tab titled — deadpan — **"How to upload myself to the internet."** A cosmology, a film syllabus, a numerology workbook, and a self-upload feasibility study, all in one sheet. Roy posted an image of it publicly on his birthday in 2026, captioned *"It's Your Time,"* which is what makes it citable here. See [[The I'm 33 Board]] for the full guided tour.

- **The board itself.** The term, the film list, the seven Hermetic principles transcribed verbatim, the cosmogonic poem, the "Tracks" list, and a stack of parallel ten-stage encodings all live here. *"As above, so below… The formula is the same from a solar system down to the atom."* And — because this is a person's actual spreadsheet, not a finished treatise — the cosmic apparatus keeps getting interrupted by the comedy of the man making it: a **kangaroo in a hoodie** pasted into the cosmology images with no explanation; comic-koan one-liners filed beside the rose-cross (*"I'm an egg, in the process of becoming a chicken to make more eggs…"*; *"Maybe a planet is one star's way of becoming another star"*; *"I love peeing and I love trees"*); and a straight-faced derivation of *the greatest song ever written* that works through *"what is music?"* and lands, deadpan, on **Happy Birthday.** The board is a cosmology *and* a sketchbook, and it never decides which.
- **[[Down to You]] (official video, 2021).** Years before anyone had seen the board, the axiom was already public. The end-card — the third of Roy's [[The Manifesto Cards|manifesto cards]] — prints **"AS ABOVE SO BELOW"** as a sigil, in a triad with *"NONE"* and the Jagwar Twin glyph, flanking the question *"WHAT IS IT THAT YOU SEEK?"* Beside it: *"To change the world one must first change the concept of the world. To do that one must change the concept of one's self. The concept one holds of the self makes one see the world as they do."* That manifesto has a native home: the **thechaosorder.academy** microsite (the *Down to You* site, 2021; recovered via Wayback), which carries the same paragraph plus a line the end-card omits — *"No one else can be IT, you must be IT for your own unique journey."* It's near-verbatim **Neville Goddard** (*"change your conception of yourself and you change your world"*), which is what makes the Goddard attribution a live *lead* rather than a guess — though it still wants checking against a Goddard primary. The Hermetic thread isn't new — it's been load-bearing for five years. The song reads the axiom *personally:* *"it all comes down to you"* (with the counter-direction ad-lib *"(up from below)"*) makes *you* one of the One's own perspectives, and the exchange runs both ways — *"to your mind, a light will find its way from heaven."* As-above-so-below isn't a one-way ladder; it's a reversible heaven↔earth swap, which is exactly what a true correspondence has to be. See [[The Visual Canon Throughline]].
- **[[Another Way II Heaven]] (*33*, 2022, track 8).** This is the single most grounded song-to-Pattern link in the catalogue, because the board's cosmogonic poem *is* this song — the same creation myth composed twice, once as text and once as a recording. *"I spoke the words 'One become Two'… A Darkness and a Light… when we crashed and came together, A Sun was born inside… one day We'll get back together and see The Great Design."* When the creation-myth-as-self-portrait reading shows up elsewhere in this guide, this is where it's anchored.
- **[[Soul Is A Star]] (*33*).** On the board, the song is drafted with *"Sun"* — *"who had a Sun / my Sun was a singer"* — against the released spelling *"Son."* A homophone hiding in plain sight, coherent with the board's *"the sun = symbol of manifesting energy, externalized Life Force."* The released lyric is "Son"; the board's "Sun" stays an artifact detail, not a correction.
- **Roy's public feed (@JagwarTwin).** The crow/Mercury disclaimer (2026); *"The rose blooms in the center of the cross"* (2026), anchoring the board's Rosicrucian cluster; and a 2023 post quoting Walter Russell's *The Universal One* verbatim — *"All commonplace realities of to-day are the miracles of yesterday's dreaming. The realist crucifies genius…"* — which is, in effect, [[Soul Is A Star]] retold in someone else's words.
- **[[Hall of Mirrors]] (web3 ARG, 2022– ).** Several of the board's "Tracks" titles are realized as unlock-codes in the maze: **Marduk** unlocks an artifact (*"Marduk. You've got to pay Attention,"* Roy confirms), **"Let Them Eat Cake"** becomes the code **CAKE** (*"Caution, All Kings End. CAKE"*), and **O**, **Genesis**, and **As Above So Below** recur as gallery codes. The ARG uses the board as its answer-quarry.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — one ascent, many encodings
> The board reads less like a loose collage than like *one* schema rendered in parallel alphabets — which is exactly what "the Pattern" would predict of itself. A single ten-stage ascent (descent and return) is written out four ways: in **tone** (an F→B note scale), in **color** (a ten-stage light ascent), in **virtue and vice** (ten positive/negative traits), and in the **body** (*"Body Part Stages": 1 Feet … 5 Heart … 10 Crown*, with Japanese stage-labels running 身体 *body* → 最高の自己 *highest self*). The same arc then gets container-figures: the **Tree of Life**, a hopscotch **Jacob's Ladder** from Earth (1) to Heaven (marked *"NChSh,"* Nachash, the serpent), the **Hero's Journey wheel** (*"Ordinary World → Rabbit Hole → … → Master of Twin Worlds → New Cycle Begins"*) mapped onto *"cycles of human consciousness throughout history,"* and a 0–10 emanation list (*0 "Nothing IS" … 6 "The mirror of the Soul and the heart" … 10 "The World"*). The strong inference: these aren't separate systems, they're one structure shown many times.
>
> A finer structural reading: two of the encodings *disagree on where the Heart sits.* The somatic ladder puts *"5 Heart"* dead center, with the throat/utterance-gate just above it. The 0–10 emanation list puts *"I speak the word"* at stage 2 — *before* the heart, which doesn't appear until stage 6. The board never flags the two ladders as conflicting, so reading the mismatch as anything is interpretation — but read structurally, that mismatch *is* the catalogue's "my mind must serve my heart" tension drawn twice, two different ways: one diagram where the heart governs the voice, one where the word comes first. The Heart/Mind problem, encoded into the architecture.

## Source notes

- **Grounded — Roy's own term and frame.** "The Pattern" is written verbatim on the publicly posted "I'm 33 this year" board, in the Hermetic-correspondence cell (*"The Pattern is present on all levels"*). The board also carries the 22-film list, the seven Kybalion principles transcribed in full, the cosmogonic poem, the "Tracks" list, and the parallel ten-stage encodings. The crow/Mercury disclaimer, the rose-cross line, and the verbatim Walter Russell quote are public X posts. The "AS ABOVE SO BELOW" sigil is the [[Down to You]] (2021) end-card. [[Another Way II Heaven]] is the released song that *is* the board poem — the single most grounded song-to-Pattern link. The Hall of Mirrors codes (Marduk, CAKE, et al.) are Roy-confirmed for Marduk and CAKE.
- **Strong readings.** "One ascent, many encodings"; "One become Two" as the Roy/Lucius split written as cosmology; the four strongest film-fits as a *cluster*. Built on grounded anchors, but interpretive.
- **Plausible / weaker readings — held loosely.** The individual film pairings; the "two ladders disagree on where the Heart sits" mismatch as the Heart/Mind tension; the Gurdjieff Law-of-Seven reading of the scale; "forever = entering the Pattern." Roy's engagement with the underlying material is grounded; these specific correspondences are not.
- **The central open question.** Does the Pattern *generate* the catalogue, or is it Roy's after-the-fact map of it? Per-song mapping needs per-song anchors — never back-fit. The honest version of this page also names where the principles *don't* illuminate, not only where they do; a reading is only worth its strong tier if it would fail on some songs. The Neville Goddard attribution remains a lead to verify against a Goddard primary — firmed (not closed) by the recovery of the *thechaosorder.academy* microsite, which carries the concept-of-self manifesto and the *"you must be IT"* line at a dated 2021 anchor.
