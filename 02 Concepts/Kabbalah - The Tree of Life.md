# Kabbalah - The Tree of Life

> **One-line reading.** The Tree of Life is the diagram the Jagwar Twin world keeps drawing without naming it: one source of light and a row of bodies that only reflect it — which turns out to be the exact shape of Roy, Lucius, and Mary.

## At a glance

- **In one line** — a map of how a single divine light pours down through ten stages into the world; in the songs, the structural skeleton under who reflects, who shines, and who breaks.
- **Where it shows up** — [[Another Way II Heaven]] (Roy's "One become Two" creation poem) · [[Bounce]] (the title card quotes the *Sefer Yetzirah*) · [[No. 1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]] (the pillar-vs-pillar takedown).
- **Connected ideas** — [[Source vs Reflection]] · [[Keilim - The Breaking of the Vessels]] · [[The Light-Bearer]] · [[The Angels and the Angles]] · [[SOL]].
- **Confidence** — Roy's documented engagement is grounded; the specific placements (who sits where on the Tree) are a framework lens the songs fit, not doctrine the artist has stated.

## What it is

Kabbalah is the mystical tradition of Judaism, and its signature diagram is the **Tree of Life** — a map of how one infinite source pours itself "downward" into the created world through ten stages. Those stages are the **sefirot**: vessels, or filters, each catching a measure of the original light and passing it on. The cleanest picture is a single sun with a row of mirrors hung below it. Only the sun makes light. Everything beneath it shines by reflection — and the whole drama of this catalogue lives in the difference between *being* a light and *reflecting* one.

This is not a frame imposed from outside. Roy openly studies this material. His "I'm 33" board lists the Tree of Life and the *sefirot* among its sources and uses the ten-stage ascent as its documented backbone; he wears the Hebrew phrase *El Roi* as a tattoo; he quotes Dion Fortune (who wrote *The Mystical Qabalah*) by name on a public quote-card. So the tradition is genuinely his. What's a lens — clearly labeled as such throughout — is the move of assigning each character a seat on the Tree.

The ten sefirot, briefly, with the ones that matter most here:

- **Keter** — the Crown; the highest point, nearest the infinite source. The crown that did *not* shatter (hold onto this; "reclaim your crown" turns on it).
- **Binah** — Understanding; the Great Mother, the womb the lower world is born from.
- **Tiferet** — Beauty; the heart of the Tree, its balanced center. In the sun-and-mirrors picture, **Tiferet is the sun** — the source the lower vessels reflect, and the pole the song [[SOL]] (Spanish for *sun*) points at.
- **Netzach** — Victory; tied to Venus — love, beauty, art, devotion ([[Roy]]'s sphere).
- **Hod** — Splendour; intellect and strategy.
- **Yesod** — Foundation; the **reflective sphere**, the realm of images, sometimes called the Treasure House of Images. It makes no light of its own; it relays what's above it downward.
- **Malkhut** — Kingdom; the lowest sefirah, the created world we live in. Also called the **Shekhinah** — the indwelling divine presence, the feminine face of God *in exile*, a moon reflecting a light that isn't its own and longing to be restored.

The sefirot also stand in **three vertical columns, or "pillars."** Right is Mercy, left is Severity (judgment), and the central pillar is balance and return — the one that holds the other two in equilibrium. Keep the pillars in view; half the character drama is who's standing on which one.

## How it works

Read through the Tree, the central figures stop looking like a cast and start looking like one source and its reflections. The governing question — does a reflection lead you back to the source, or trap you in the image? — belongs to [[Source vs Reflection]]; the Tree is just the structure that question runs on.

**The sun is the heart.** **Tiferet — the balanced center — is the heart-pole** the whole catalogue orbits. It's the one light that makes its own light. Everything else on the axis is a body that doesn't.

**Roy is the moon's *position*.** Roy occupies Malkhut and Yesod — the only figure standing on *both* sefirot that either tradition calls lunar, the reflective vessel that receives entirely from the sun. The recurring image of *"the moon trying to become a sun"* is **his** arc: the diminished vessel reaching to be restored to full light. It's the structure under *"reclaim your crown"* — and crucially, Roy stands *alone* on the central pillar of balance and return, which is the structural reason he's the one who passes through the pressure and can still say *"I am still I."*

**Mary is the moon's *theology*.** [[Mary]], the divine-feminine presence of the songs, reads as the Shekhinah — the reflected divine light in exile, longing to return. Where Roy holds the moon's position, Mary holds its personhood and its ascent. Her line *"saw a halo but I didn't see a head"* reads as the crown reached at the top of the climb: present, but unpossessable.

**Lucius is the intellect — Hod / Mercury.** [[Sir Lucius]] maps to **Hod (Mercury)**, the analytic mind, married to **Geburah** severity — with a buried Lucifer / morning-star (Venus) resonance held separate from [[Roy]]'s Venus: the green-apple intelligence-divorced-from-spirit, truth bent by framing. His *operating theater* is Yesod, the realm of images — the glamour he works *through*. So the project's broken imagery — an artificial apple, a sterile banana — reads as **Yesod gone wrong:** the image-realm producing reflections that have forgotten their source.

So the reflecting side of the Tree holds two bodies — the Moon (Roy, with Mary carrying its theology) and the usurping reflection (Lucius — the morning-star a demoted resonance) — and the Sun at the center is the light all three orbit and **none of them is.**

**The pillars sharpen it.** Mary (at Binah, the head of the Severity pillar) and Lucius (at Hod/Geburah, lower on that same pillar) share the *left* column — so when Mary "drops Lucius dead" in [[No. 1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]], it reads not as a tidy Mercy-vs-Severity duel but as the *origin* of judgment overruling the shadow-intelligence beneath it on its own pillar. Lucius, meanwhile, raids Netzach (right/Mercy) from his Hod/Geburah base — cross-pillar by design. And Roy, alone on the middle, is the only one in equilibrium.

**Where the Tree breaks.** There's a companion idea the project leans on just as hard. In the Lurianic strand of Kabbalah, the original vessels couldn't hold the light poured into them and **shattered** — a primordial catastrophe called the *breaking of the vessels*. The repair, gathering the scattered sparks, is **tikkun**, the healing of the world. That's the deep shape under *"my mind must serve my heart"*: the shattered Tree is a set of faculties come apart and stopped cooperating; the repair is re-ordering them — the morning-star's mind brought back under the heart-sun, the moon restored to the sun's light. Structure (the Tree) and fracture (the breaking) are two halves of one doctrine — which is why this page has a companion, [[Keilim - The Breaking of the Vessels]].

One honest caution lives here, and it has to survive into any reading: Lurianic Kabbalah is **world-affirming** — the broken world is to be *redeemed*, the sparks raised where they fell. That's a different impulse from the *escape*-the-world frame the project sometimes sounds (see [[Gnosticism - The World as Trap]]). The two share a lot of imagery — descent, exile, return — but they point in opposite directions, and the work holds both in tension rather than choosing. Keep them distinct. Never silently merge the destinations.

## Where it appears

The Tree isn't a reading laid over silent songs. The work keeps printing the furniture itself — and, just as often, smuggling it in singsong.

**The nursery-rhyme method (the joke that's actually structure).** *Lucius Lullaby* states its own technique out loud: cosmology delivered as a children's chant. *"Mary, Mary, so contrary"* (#1 Champion), *"ring around the rosies"* (Not Your Homie), *"Row, row, row your boat / life is but a dream"* (Lucius Lullaby), *"Oompa Loompa, doompa-de-doo"* (Bad Feeling), *"go bananananana"* — the whole Tree-of-Life apparatus poured into the simplest, stickiest forms we know. This is the producer's stated principle ("the simplest songs are the most complex") made into a method, and it's the reason the heavy esoterica never reads as homework: the demiurge is hiding inside a playground rhyme. It is, structurally, the most important thing on this list — not a gag laid over the cosmology but the *delivery system for it.*

- **The "I'm 33" board** — Roy's own. It names *"Tree of Life / sephirot,"* sketches "A Head of Adam," draws an inverted golden Tree (crown plus triangle), and carries the Kabbalistic veils in his hand: *"Ring Pass-Not / Ring Chaos (Ain Soph) / Ring Cosmos (Ain),"* the "4 worlds," "Genesis 1:26," and a Noah's Ark cell built on *"11 sections / 3 stories (Heaven, Man, Earth) / 32."* Its backbone is a handwritten **0–10 emanation list** — verbatim Tree glosses — whose stage 6 reads *"The mirror of the Soul and the heart."* That's the heart-pole, in Roy's own hand. (Full decode: [[The I'm 33 Board]].)
- **[[Bounce]] (Live Underground)** — an official @jagwartwin video whose opening title card epigraphs the *Sefer Yetzirah*'s Beth passage: *"HE MADE THE LETTER BETH KING OVER WISDOM. HE BOUND A CROWN TO IT… AND WITH THEM HE FORMED THE MOON IN THE UNIVERSE…"* (Aryeh Kaplan's translation). Kabbalistic letter-mysticism, printed directly on the work.
- **[[Another Way II Heaven]]** (33, 2022, track 8; Roy sole writer) — the *same* cosmogonic "One become Two" poem Roy laid out on the board. Composed twice, which is the cleanest evidence the creation-myth reading is Roy's own framing, not a reader's projection. It runs *"from 1:00 and through 11:00,"* with the return to 12 deferred to "The Great Design."
- **[[No. 1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]]** — Mary named *and* faceless (*"saw a halo but I didn't see a head"*); the visualizer caption signed *"— El Roy."*
- **Lucius's *El Roi* throat tattoo** — Hebrew (אל ראי), *"the God who sees me"* (Genesis 16:13), inked on the throat in *The Interview* set and a head-tilted video still. Roy also signs public captions *"El Roy."*
- **[[Ballerina Boy]]** — the spoken outro, in an older motherly voice: *"the moon reminds us that even in the darkness there is light… my son, let your light shine."* The clearest Mary-class divine-feminine appearance.
- **The "reclaim your crown" statement** — Roy's 33 release copy (2022), signed *"El Roy the Jagwar"*: *"I pray that this album serves you as a mirror and a reminder of who you are… Even in the darkest places, there is light. Kings and Queens, it's time to reclaim your crown!"*
- **Roy's tattoos** — composed as a Tree-of-Life mirror diagram: every mark inked on the right side then mirrored to the left, with one *"+"* spanning both. (Body-as-Tree; see the Advanced reading.)
- **Roy's 2019 Facebook "Diary of a loser" post** — *"fell asleep watching a documentary on the Hebrew language. It's a cool language… Everything is a mirror."* Grounds his Hebrew *interest*, and that one phrase — *everything is a mirror* — is practically the page's thesis in his own words.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — where the Tree breaks (Keilim / Shevirat HaKelim)
> In Lurianic Kabbalah (Isaac Luria, the Arizal, 1534–1572, via Chaim Vital's *Etz Chaim*), the sequence runs *Ein Sof* → *tzimtzum* (the divine contraction that makes room) → the *kav* (the thread of light) → *Adam Kadmon* → the vessels (*kelim*) → **Shevirat HaKelim**, the breaking of the vessels that couldn't hold the light → *kelipot* and *nitzotzot* (husks and scattered sparks) → *partzufim* (re-configured "faces") → **tikkun**, the repair. The shattered worlds are ciphered as the "Kings of Edom."
>
> *"My mind must serve my heart"* **is** that re-ordering made into an ethic: the morning-star's mind (Hod/Netzach) re-subordinated to the heart-Sun (Tiferet); *Olam HaTohu* (isolated, shattered, "couldn't share") → *Olam HaTikkun* (a related configuration). The breaking-of-the-vessels reading rests entirely on public anchors: *"I'm just a vessel for God"* (the self as *keli*), the sterile banana (a husk that holds form but cannot seed), the *El Roi* tattoo, the scattered light of [[City of Angels]], and *"reclaim your crown."*
>
> The repair is *not* a single move, and the arc keeps three live at once — by stream, never merged: a Lurianic *necessary break* asks you to **re-order** (tikkun); a Lurianic *kelipah / husk* asks you to **stop and strip**; a Jungian *shadow* asks you to **redeem and integrate** (Steiner's Dweller-who-becomes-guide). Which is why Roy's public *"STOP SIR LUCIUS"* rebellion collapses on contact — Lucius is a misplaced power, not an enemy to kill — and why the redemption question stays open *by design.*

> [!note]- Advanced reading — reflection has a graded interior (transmission → distortion → usurpation)
> A reflection isn't simply true or false; it has a graded interior. **Transmission** (the reflection serves its source) → **Distortion** (it forgets it's a reflection) → **Usurpation** (it tries to *become* the source). Lucius is usurpation: *"the moon trying to become a sun"* at full strength isn't the moon failing to shine — it's the moon staging a coup on the sun, the *kelipah* (husk) that substitutes for the source and then claims to *be* it.
>
> Which sets up the one limit the usurpation can't cross. **SOL** — the wordless Song of Love, Tiferet's own signal — is the single thing the Lucius-system can suppress and reduce but cannot *absorb* (*"he reaches for a thing with no surface to grip"*). The reflection-claiming-sourcehood meets a source it has no instrument to convert. So SOL *survives* Lucius rather than conquering him — which matters: exposing a limit is not the same as winning a fight, and the work never pretends it is.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the two axes of Kabbalah (keep them separate)
> *Sefer Yetzirah* unites *"22 foundation letters + 10 sefirot = 32 paths of wisdom."* That's two systems sharing one text, and they should not be merged:
> 1. **The emanation structure** — sefirot and pillars. *This* page.
> 2. **The letter-mysticism** — [[The Angels and the Angles]]: consonants as the *angles* (the body / structure), vowels as the *angels* (breath / *Ruach* / spirit), with Abulafia's permutation-with-vowel-tones as *"a ladder for the mind to climb."*
>
> The lineage behind axis 2, as public scholarship — the documented tradition *under* the method, not the compositional method itself (which Roy has stated as his own; see [[The Angels and the Angles]]): *Sefer Yetzirah* (22 letters split into 3 mothers / 7 doubles / 12 simples; the Beth passage that forms the Moon); Abraham Abulafia's ecstatic *tzeruf*; the doctrine that vowels = spirit and consonants = body (Zohar Bereshit 15b, *"the letters are the body and the vowel-points the soul"*; Ezra ben Solomon of Gerona; Gikatilla's *Sefer HaNikud*); letters as angels via the Shem HaMephorash (Exodus 14:19–21 → the 72 three-letter Names). One sequencing note, so the parallels aren't mis-cited as a template: Ezekiel 37 assembles the *body first*, then summons *ruach* last; the catalogue runs it the other way — breath/vowel-spirit first, verbal structure after. Same doctrine, reversed order.
>
> A weakened sub-hypothesis, recorded honestly: a predicted *Bounce → Beth* letter-cipher (that the Live Underground title cards would spell out Hebrew letters) is disconfirmed in its strict form — the "Welcome to the Circus" card carried a Hermes riddle, not the predicted letter. What survives is the modest claim: *a song-specific esoteric epigraph per card, drawn from the Hermetic-Kabbalah well.* The load-bearing find is the **Beth = house = heart** convergence on the *Bounce* card; the Babel wordplay is demoted, re-scoped to the Lucius Lullaby wave.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the Hermetic Qabalah and the Tree of Death (kept separate)
> The Western Mystery tradition (Knorr von Rosenroth → Mathers → the Golden Dawn) systematized a demonic mirror-Tree, the **Qliphoth** or "Tree of Death," with the *Qliphah of Keter* (Thaumiel) as the counterfeit crown. Different teloi. The rule that survives into every reading: *Lurianic kelipot ≠ Hermetic Qliphoth, and Lurianic (world-affirming) ≠ Gnostic (anti-cosmic). Always label the stream.*

The honest test, kept on the table: does each Tree placement *add* explanatory power, or just decorate a doctrine already complete without it?
