# Hermeticism - As Above So Below

> **One-line reading.** Hermeticism is the opposite of treating the world as a trap: it holds the cosmos as one coherent, legible law — the same pattern repeating at every scale — and Roy didn't just study it, he printed its central maxim on a music video years before anyone decoded his sources.

## At a glance

- **In one line** — *as above, so below*: one design echoing through every level of reality, from the galaxy down to the atom — a cosmos you can read and climb rather than flee.
- **Where it shows up** — [[Down to You]] (the reversible heaven↔earth refrain; *"AS ABOVE SO BELOW"* printed on the 2021 video end-card) · [[Life Is Good]] (*"my head's an antenna"*) · [[Welcome to the Circus]] (*"Did Hermes paint his face?"*).
- **Connected ideas** — [[The Pattern]] · [[Source vs Reflection]] · [[Sir Lucius]] · [[Duality]] · [[Inversion]] · [[Mary]] · [[Gnosticism - The World as Trap]] · [[Kabbalah - The Tree of Life]].
- **Confidence** — Roy's *engagement* with Hermeticism is grounded fact (the books are on his shelf, the maxim is on his video, the seven principles are transcribed in his own hand). The song-by-song *mappings* are interpretation — a frame the work fits, not a proven blueprint.

## What it is

Hermeticism is a Western esoteric tradition rooted in late-antique texts attributed to a legendary sage, **Hermes Trismegistus** — the *Corpus Hermeticum*, or *Hermetica*. Its single most famous idea is a maxim from the **Emerald Tablet**: *as above, so below*. Whatever pattern holds at the largest scale — the cosmos, the divine — repeats at every smaller one: the world, the body, the mind. The universe isn't a deceptive copy to escape. It's a coherent, lawful order you can *read* and *climb*, one design echoing through every level.

Most of the tradition's modern reach comes from **The Kybalion** (1908), a popular primer that boils it down to **seven principles**:

1. **Mentalism** — "The All is Mind." Reality is, at bottom, mental; the universe is a thought.
2. **Correspondence** — "as above, so below." The same pattern recurs at every scale. (This is the famous one.)
3. **Vibration** — nothing rests; everything is motion, everything is a frequency.
4. **Polarity** — everything is dual; opposites are two ends of one thing.
5. **Rhythm** — everything swings, like a pendulum, between its poles.
6. **Cause and Effect** — every cause has its effect; nothing is mere chance.
7. **Gender** — a masculine and a feminine principle operate in everything.

The throughline is *coherence*: a legible cosmos, not a hostile one. And here's the part that lifts this out of armchair theory — Roy put **both** the *Hermetica* and *The Kybalion* on his public Instagram Book Club reading list. The books are literally on the shelf. He also posted a quote-card crediting Hermes Trismegistus by name (*"The center is everywhere"*), naming the tradition directly rather than leaving us to infer it.

## How it works

Hermeticism is the most board-grounded of the three esoteric streams the project draws on, because Roy transcribed all seven Kybalion principles — verbatim, in full — onto his "I'm 33 this year" spreadsheet, on a tab cheerfully titled *"How to upload myself to the internet."* (Yes. The cosmology and the upload fantasy live on the same browser tab.) That gives us an unusual luxury: we can watch the principles move through the work one by one, with the source material sitting right there next to it.

**Mentalism** — *"The All is Mind"* — is the premise under the project's whole consciousness-and-upload imagery. The board's "Cybernetics" cell ends on the cleanest possible statement of it: *"All the world is mind. Mind woven into matter."* If reality is fundamentally information, then a self is information too — and information can, in principle, be externalized. That single move is the engine of the entire upload cosmology (see [[The Upload]], [[The Digital Double]]).

**Correspondence** — *"as above, so below"* — is the principle Roy engages most directly, so directly that he gave it his own name: **The Pattern**. The board's Correspondence cell reads, in part, *"The formula is the same from a solar system down to the atom"* — and then lists Adam, Hurricane, Galaxy, Fibonacci, closing on *"The Pattern is present on all levels."* That's the central maxim turned into a working term. It's also the logic the mirror runs on: [[Source vs Reflection]] is *as above, so below* applied to a single act of mediation — what's above, faithfully reflected below.

**Vibration** is the principle with the most public anchors, which makes it the firmest ground on the page. Three of them: the board cell quotes Tesla verbatim (*"If you wish to understand the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency and vibration"*) and closes on *"Tune to desired frequency"*; Roy restated the doctrine in his own voice in a 2020 Q&A (*"Everything is a vibrational frequency. Light, matter, sound, it's all the same thing at variable speeds"*); and the released lyric *"My head's an antenna / I'm wide awake"* ([[Life Is Good]]) is the principle in first person — mind as a receiver tuned to a frequency. Three anchors lift this past coincidence into documented engagement. The principle also has two darker registers, both in released songs (more below).

**Polarity** surfaces as the project's deep concern with **duality** — the Roy/Lucius split, the "one becomes two" shape of the whole story (see [[Duality]]). The Kybalion teaches that opposites are two ends of *one* thing, which is why the work's resolution is never to defeat a pole but to re-order the pair. [[Life Is Good]] even enacts the refusal: *"Keep a smile on my face, too happy to choose"* declines the this-side/that-side split outright.

The remaining three principles map more lightly, by resonance: **Rhythm** to the Circle/Loop recurrence (see [[The Circle]]); **Cause and Effect** to the seed-and-karma material; **Gender** to the divine-feminine thread ([[Mary]]) and the board's own Left/Right-hemisphere cell (Order/reason/masculine vs. Chaos/creativity/feminine).

And then there's the figure who carries the tradition's *name* into the masks: **[[Sir Lucius]] as Hermes.** The 2026 *welcome to the circus (Live Underground)* card appended a new line to the chorus — *"BUT WHO ARE YOU WITHOUT THE MASK? DID HERMES PAINT HIS FACE? I DON'T KNOW, MAYBE."* — surfacing the Hermetic thread publicly again, five years after the *Down to You* card. Hermes is the trickster, the psychopomp, the inventor of writing and letters, the namesake of both *Hermeticism* and *hermeneutics* (the art of decoding). It's a near-perfect fit for the face-painting ringmaster of a "Live UNDERGROUND" series — and the *"I don't know, maybe"* performs the cipher's own shrug. The card is the anchor; the full equation is a reading.

## Where it appears

- **[[Down to You]]** — the public floor. The 2021 *Down to You (Official Music Video)* end-card printed **"AS ABOVE SO BELOW"** as a sigil, flanking the seeker-question *"WHAT IS IT THAT YOU SEEK?"* — years before Roy's source board was decoded. The song's mutating refrain stages the maxim as a *reversible exchange*: *"Heaven in the sky to the Earth below / Heaven in your eye from the Earth below,"* with the ad-lib *"up from below."* Descent **and** ascent — the circuit completed, not a one-way drop. (The title *"it all comes down to you"* doubles as responsibility and as emanation: the divine descending *to you.*)
- **[[Life Is Good]]** (JT × angelbaby, 2023) — *"My head's an antenna / I'm wide awake"* (Vibration, first person) and *"Keep a smile on my face, too happy to choose"* (Polarity, refusing the pole-pair).
- **[[Online]]** (*33*, 2022) — *"And they know my every step / They infiltrate my brain."* The Kybalion's induction/telepathy clause — *"mind over mind"* — inverted from gift into violation; the Vibration doctrine run as surveillance-horror.
- **[[Loser]]** (*Subject to Flooding*, 2018) — *"Jericho fell like a wave."* Frequency turned destructive: the wall that falls to a sound, vibration that dismantles containment.
- **[[Welcome to the Circus]]** (*Lucius Lullaby*, 2026) — the Hermetic thread named (*"Did Hermes paint his face?"*) and inverted (*"Everything's upside down"*).
- **The "I'm 33" board** — the seven principles transcribed in full; the source of the term *The Pattern*; and a typed *"Tracks"* list (a likely-unreleased concept album running a Hermetic descent/return arc) whose titles recur as [[Hall of Mirrors]] unlock-codes.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — Vibration's two dark registers (Online, Loser)
> The Kybalion's Vibration cell carries a telepathy clause: thought and emotion are *"accompanied by vibrations… which tend to affect the minds of other persons by induction… telepathy… mind over mind."* That's framed as a gift — minds tuning to each other. Two released songs take the same machinery and run it backwards.
>
> **Online** inverts induction into **surveillance**: *"they infiltrate my brain"* is mind-over-mind as intrusion, the telepathic field weaponized into a watcher. The comedy is load-bearing here — the song answers every transcendent "from above" claim with a deflation: *"All the shamans and the Brahmans say we come from space"* is immediately undercut by *"Maybe Jupiter is stupider than people say,"* and the whole cosmic pile-up resolves to *"we're stuck just like a bug inside your database."* The *Buy! Buy! Buy! / Bye! Bye! Bye!* homophone makes consumption and farewell the same sound — the jingle-as-sedation that lets the surveillance horror go down easy. Vibration doctrine, run as paranoia, then shrugged off. This is correspondence, not claimed intent — the song never names the principle.
>
> **Loser** takes the destructive register: *"Jericho fell like a wave"* is frequency turned to collapse — the wall that falls to a trumpet blast. The grammar the song supplies is *voices break walls; vibration dismantles containment.* The board names the principle; the line lets it ring on the wall.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the "Tracks" concept album (and the ARG corroboration)
> The board includes a typed *"Tracks"* list — a distinct, likely-unreleased concept album (not *33*): **xWebx · The Fall into Matter · Marduk · Let Them Eat Cake · Sun of God · Dissolution · As Above So Below · Genesis · The All Is Mind · O.** Read top to bottom, that's a Hermetic descent-and-return arc: spirit falling into matter, dissolving, and climbing back to *The All Is Mind*. It's the cosmology rendered as a tracklist.
>
> Here's the corroboration that the frame is *operative* and not decorative: several of those titles are realized as [[Hall of Mirrors]] ARG unlock-codes, Roy-confirmed. *Marduk* (*"You've got to pay Attention"*), *Let Them Eat Cake* (*"Caution, All Kings End. CAKE"*), plus *O*, *Genesis*, and *As Above So Below* recur as gallery artifacts. The board's Hermetic frame didn't stay on the spreadsheet — it became playable architecture. Whether any "Track" was ever recorded as a song is still open; its medium may be the maze, not a record.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — Lucius as Hermes / Thoth / Mercurius
> The 2026 *"Did Hermes paint his face?"* card recasts the face-painting ringmaster as **Hermes** — and the fit is dense. Hermes is the **trickster**; the **psychopomp** (guide through the underworld — exactly right for a "Live UNDERGROUND" series); the **inventor of writing and letters**; the namesake of both **Hermeticism** and **hermeneutics**, the art of decoding. > [!note]- Advanced reading — Hermetic INVERSION: the maxim run backwards
> [[Welcome to the Circus]] enacts a **broken** correspondence — *as below, so above* — the material treated as supreme, the supreme dragged down into the material: *"Spinning you 'round and 'round / Everything's upside down."* The Circus is the As-Above-So-Below axiom inverted. This is the same move [[Inversion]] names: matter elevated to spirit's place, the Qliphoth as a Tree-of-Death, the Hanged Man's *voluntary* inversion versus the Circus's *involuntary* one — with its scriptural signatures (Isaiah 5:20, *"call evil good"*; Acts 17:6, *"turned the world upside down"*) and the Kabbalistic *tzimtzum* (*"contract to expand"*). A proper *as above, so below* affirms the cosmos; its inversion is the demonic counterfeit.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the Hermetic-stream tarot finale (the Light-Bearer scaffold)
> The Hermetic Qabalah (Golden Dawn, not the Jewish-Lurianic stream) supplies a tarot finale unique to the esoteric scaffold around Lucius: **Star (XVII)** — the morning-star, Venus-Lucifer — → **Moon (XVIII)** — reflection, illusion — → **Sun (XIX)** — the true light. Lucius as Venus, the herald of dawn, outshone by the Sun he only preceded (→ SOL). This finale is kept openly interpretive; the **Devil card (XV)** is the only *grounded* tarot anchor for Lucius (Roy named it himself, via Paul Foster Case). See [[The Light-Bearer]].

> [!note]- Advanced reading — parallel, not nested: As-Above-So-Below vs Source-vs-Reflection
> Two ideas that look like one. **As-Above-So-Below** is *correspondence of structure across scales* (the same shape, galaxy to atom). **[[Source vs Reflection]]** is *fidelity of a mediation* (whether a reflection is true to its source). They **overlap** where a faithful reflection *is* a true As-Above-So-Below; they **part** where a distorted reflection is a *broken* correspondence that the Pattern-as-structure doesn't itself name. They rhyme by shared theosophical ancestry, not necessarily by deliberate cross-encoding. Hold them as parallel siblings, not parent and child.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — stream discipline (the non-negotiable guardrails)
> Three distinctions that have to stay clean, because the same imagery (descent, ascent, the spark) can carry opposite meanings depending on which tradition you're standing in:
>
> - **Hermetic ≠ Gnostic.** Hermeticism is **world-affirming** (the cosmos is a coherent law, legible and climbable). [[Gnosticism - The World as Trap]] is **world-rejecting** (the cosmos is a false copy to flee). Same descent-and-return imagery, *opposite telos.* Always label the stream.
> - **Hermetic ≠ Lurianic.** The **Hermetic Qabalah** (Golden Dawn, tarot, Qliphoth-as-Tree-of-Death) is *not* the **Jewish-Lurianic Kabbalah** ([[Kabbalah - The Tree of Life]], [[Keilim - The Breaking of the Vessels]]). Different "Tree," different lineage.
> - **Engagement ≠ architecture.** That Roy *transcribed* the seven principles proves he read and collected the Kybalion — a reading-list, not a proven compositional grammar. The leap from "he wrote it down" to "the songs run on it" is interpretation, and labeled as such.
>
> The wider substrate the Kybalion sits inside — Walter Russell (octaves, the cosmic pendulum), Alice Bailey (the *Labours of Hercules* material in Roy's hand; see [[The Labors of Hercules]]), Plato's *Timaeus* World-Soul, Jung — is what makes the vault's concepts naturally rhyme. The Kybalion is board-grounded; the off-board Golden-Dawn correspondences stay interpretive. See [[Inherited Substrate]].

Roy supplies the ceiling himself, defusing his own method with a straight face: *"I would map a crow onto Mercury but that's just personal. I don't recommend you do it."* The author runs the correspondence-system **and** disclaims it in the same breath — which is exactly the honesty this page tries to keep. Note where the principles *don't* illuminate, not only where they do.
