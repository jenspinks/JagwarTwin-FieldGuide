# The Manifesto Cards

> **One-line reading.** The Manifesto Cards are the project's recurring title-card device — a flyer-like graphic that hides a tiny esoteric manifesto in its fine print, so the casual scroller sees an ad while the attentive viewer finds a thesis.

Since roughly 2019, a number of Jagwar Twin releases have carried the same graphic device: a mock credit-card or title-card, styled like a vintage concert flyer, "presented by" or "brought to you by Jagwar Twin." It looks like promotion. But the small type on it almost always carries a compressed statement of belief — a manifesto deliberately set too small to read at a glance. You only find it if you stop and zoom in. The concealment *is* the invitation: the card filters for the audience willing to decode.

## The template

The cards share a consistent kit of parts:

- a **blackletter title** and an era-tag (e.g. *"LIVE UNDERGROUND IN LOS ANGELES"*)
- a **"presented by / brought to you by Jagwar Twin"** frame (sometimes punning it to *"brought to you **buy**"*)
- a **seeker-question** aimed at the viewer (*"what is it that you seek?"*, *"are you content?"*, *"who are you without the mask?"*)
- a small cluster of **sigils** or slogans
- a **micro-type manifesto-paragraph** that resolves only when you stop and look closely

## Two waves

The device clusters in two periods. An early run from around 2019–2021 carried *consumerist* and Hermetic manifestos: *Happy Face* (2020), *I Like to Party* (2021), and *Down to You* (2021). It then paused for several years — the narrative videos of the middle period didn't use it — and returned in 2026 for the *Lucius Lullaby* era's *Live Underground* cards (*Bounce*, *Welcome to the Circus*).

A few of the cards, with their payloads:

- ***Happy Face*** — *"are you content?"*, with a manifesto about *"burning the old world down… the death of my generation."*
- ***I Like to Party*** — *"the Devil waking up to claim the kingdom… you're the King."*
- ***Down to You*** — *"what is it that you seek?"*, answered by *"NONE"* and *"as above, so below."*
- ***Welcome to the Circus*** — *"who are you without the mask?"*, with the labyrinth slogan *"you are the maze."*

## What the cards have been saying all along

Read in sequence, the cards have been carrying the project's core doctrines for years — often well before the songs and characters that later made them famous:

- **The kingdom within** — *"you're the Kingdom, you're the King"* (2021) is the same idea as *"you are the maze,"* and it echoes the Chaplin-by-way-of-Luke line *"the kingdom of God is within."*
- **The Devil / Lucius** — *"the Devil waking up to claim the kingdom"* appears on a 2021 card, two years before [[Sir Lucius]] was named in the work.
- **As above, so below** — the Hermetic axiom (see [[The Pattern]]) is on a card in 2021, the seed of the *Welcome to the Circus* Hermes references in 2026.
- **Change the concept of self** — the mirror doctrine of [[Source vs Reflection]], stated on the cards as a manifesto.
- **Burn it down, then rebirth** — *"death of my generation"* on the *Happy Face* card, later staged in the fire-close of the *Bad Feeling* video.

## "Are you content?"

The sharpest single line is *Happy Face*'s **"are you content?"**, which is a three-way pun. *Content* as in happy and satisfied. *Content* as in media to be consumed — *are you the content?*, paired with that card's *"brought to you buy."* And *content* as in complacent, contained — the exact opposite of the other cards' *"what is it that you seek?"* The whole consumerism-versus-seeker argument of the project, folded into one word. It points straight at [[The Spectacle]] and [[Consumption]].

## A caution

The cards themselves — the template, the text, the sigils, the dates — are documented in public videos. What's more of a reading is the temptation to treat them as proof of one fully pre-architected master plan. The cards' content actually *varies*, and not every release carries one, which argues against any rigid hidden cipher. They're best read as a recurring statement of values, not a locked code.
