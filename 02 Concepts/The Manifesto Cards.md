# The Manifesto Cards

> **One-line reading.** The Manifesto Cards are the project's recurring title-card device — a flyer-like graphic that hides a tiny esoteric manifesto in its fine print, so the casual scroller sees an ad while the attentive viewer who stops and zooms in finds a thesis.

## What it is

Since roughly 2019, a run of Jagwar Twin releases has carried the same graphic gag: a mock credit-card or concert title-card, blackletter type, *"presented by"* or *"brought to you by Jagwar Twin."* It looks like promotion. It is, in fact, a smuggling device. The small print on it almost always carries a compressed statement of belief — a manifesto set deliberately too small to read while you're thumbing past. You only find it if you stop and zoom in.

That's the whole design. The concealment *is* the invitation. The card filters its own audience: the casual scroller gets an ad, the attentive viewer gets a thesis, and nobody is forced into the deep end. It is the project's entire method shrunk to flyer scale — a values statement disguised as the very consumerism it's critiquing.

And it runs the disguise as comedy. The register is vintage TV infomercial — a registered-trademark *®*, a breathless *CALL NOW*, a sponsor stamp — esoteric doctrine wearing the costume of late-night junk advertising. The first card even tells you to buy from a misspelling: *"brought to you **buy** jagwar twin."* The sponsor-frame literally instructs you to purchase. That's not a typo anybody missed.

## How it works

The cards are a fixed kit of parts, mixed and matched per release:

- a **blackletter title** plus an era-tag (e.g. *"LIVE UNDERGROUND IN LOS ANGELES"*);
- a **"presented by / brought to you by Jagwar Twin"** frame (sometimes punned to *"brought to you **buy**"*);
- a **seeker-question** pointed at the viewer (*"what is it that you seek?"*, *"are you content?"*, *"who are you without the mask?"*);
- a **sigil-triad** — a small cluster of glyphs and slogans;
- a flanking **parenthetical echo** in the typography — *(TICK)…(TOCK)* on *Happy Face*, *(bounce)…(bounce)*, *(welcome)…(welcome)* — the card winking at its own rhythm;
- and the payload: a **micro-type manifesto-paragraph** that only resolves when you stop and look closely.

What the device *does* across the work is more interesting than the kit. Read in sequence, the cards turn out to have been carrying the project's core doctrines for years — frequently well before the songs and characters that later made those doctrines famous. The card is where the mythology shows up early and quietly, in print nobody was reading.

- **The kingdom within.** *"you're the Kingdom, you're the King"* (*I Like to Party*, 2021) is the same move as *Welcome to the Circus*'s *"you are the maze"* (2026) — the self as sovereign, the self as the structure you're navigating. It rhymes with [[Superhuman]]'s sampled Chaplin line, *"the kingdom of God is within man."*
- **The Devil / Lucius, before the name.** *"Devil waking up to claim the kingdom"* sits on a 2021 card — roughly two years before [[Sir Lucius]] arrives by name in the 2023 dream-origin. Consistent with the project's habit: a figure accumulates in public long before it's credited.
- **As above, so below.** The Hermetic axiom (see [[The Pattern]]) is a *Down to You* card sigil in 2021 — the five-year-old root of *Welcome to the Circus*'s 2026 *"Did Hermes paint his face?"* The Hermetic thread isn't new to the *Lucius Lullaby* era; it was printed in the fine print all along.
- **Change the concept of self.** The *Down to You* manifesto — *"to change the world one must first change the concept of one's self"* — is the mirror doctrine of [[Source vs Reflection]] in plain language.
- **Burn it down, then rebirth.** *Happy Face*'s *"burning the old world down… out of the fire something new is being birthed. This is the death of my generation"* is the alchemical blackening (see [[Nigredo]]), later *staged* as the fire-close of the *Bad Feeling* video.

The cards also connect the work *outward* into its alternate-reality games. The same *"are you content?"* device that runs across the cards also ran on a 2020 Jagwar Twin microsite and, later, surfaced as a working code inside the [[The Hall of Mirrors|Hall of Mirrors]] ARG — the gag had a back-end (see *Advanced reading — the are-you-content easter egg*, below).

## "Are you content?"

The sharpest single line on any card is *Happy Face*'s **"are you content?"**, and it's a three-way pun doing the work of the whole project in three words.

*Content* (adjective) — happy, satisfied. The literal Happy-Face question. *Content* (noun) — media to be consumed: *are you THE content?*, the attention-economy product, pairing exactly with that card's *"brought to you buy."* And *content* (adjective again) — complacent, contained, settled inside the system: the precise opposite of the other cards' *"what is it that you seek?"*

One infomercial throwaway holds the entire consumerism-versus-seeker argument. It points straight at [[The Spectacle]], [[Consumption]], and [[The Algorithm]]. And — this is the part that's almost too neat — the pun isn't an interpreter's invention. It's grounded in Roy's own Instagram diary from around 2020, written after watching *"a documentary on A.I. merging with humans"*: *"I am holding a device currently transmitting 1's and 0's that you are reading and something labeled content. Are you content?"* The card is Roy's own joke, formalized.

## Where it appears

Short excerpts, in order:

- **[[Long Time Coming]] (remixes), ~2019** — the device's first appearance. Sigil: a hamsa (the eye-in-hand, eyes on the fingers). No seeker-line, no *"by"*-frame yet; the payload is the remix economy itself.
- **[[Happy Face]], 2020** — the card displayed on a CRT television showing an inverted, upside-down face, framed as a *"2020 JAGWAR TWIN HAPPY FACE® RELEASE."* Frame: *"BROUGHT TO YOU BUY JAGWAR TWIN"* (the *BUY* is on the card). Seeker-line: *"ARE YOU CONTENT? CALL NOW."* Payload: *"…the power to change the world. First we must each know and change ourselves. We are burning the old world down to the ground… out of the fire something new is being birthed. This is the death of my generation."*
- **[[I Like to Party]], 2021** — frame: *"PRESENTED BY JAGWAR TWIN"*; on-card barker copy *"Attention! / LET'S SEE YOU CLAP THEM HANDS!"*; sigil-triad 🙏 praying hands · ✝ cross · the JT glyph. Payload: *"Devil waking up to claim the kingdom. People waking up to claim what's in them. Heaven is within your heart… you're the King."* The cover art plates a cake as Saturn — a ringed planet ringed *again* by skulls and cherubs, with the tagline *"A NEW DIRECTION AFFECTING THE STATE OF YOUR MIND."*
- **[[Down to You]], 2021** — frame: *"PRESENTED BY JAGWAR TWIN"*; seeker-line: *"WHAT IS IT THAT YOU SEEK?"*; sigil-triad *"NONE"* · the JT glyph · *"AS ABOVE SO BELOW."* Payload: *"To change the world one must first change the concept of the world. To do that one must change the concept of one's self. The concept one holds of the self makes one see the world as they do."* (It's also a Joni Mitchell title, *Court and Spark*, 1974.)
- **[[Bounce]] (Live Underground), 2026** — the opening title sequence of the live track, not a separate poster. Era-tag *"LIVE UNDERGROUND IN LOS ANGELES"*; frame *"(bounce) BROUGHT TO YOU BY JAGWAR TWIN (bounce)"*; sigils: sunburst · *"life is short"* (also the song's own lyric) · a jaguar/skull doodle. Payload: the *Sefer Yetzirah* Beth passage verbatim — *"HE MADE THE LETTER BETH KING OVER WISDOM. HE BOUND A CROWN TO IT… AND WITH THEM HE FORMED THE MOON IN THE UNIVERSE, SUNDAY IN THE YEAR, AND THE RIGHT EYE IN THE SOUL, MALE AND FEMALE."* The first actual Hebrew letter on any card — a new move (see *Advanced reading — Beth, the house, and the roof*).
- **[[Welcome to the Circus]] (Live Underground), 2026** — frame *"(welcome) BROUGHT TO YOU BY JAGWAR TWIN (welcome)"*; left sigil a concentric-labyrinth seal reading *"you are the maze"*; right sigil the cat/twin glyph; embedded seeker-line *"who are you without the mask?"* The payload quotes the song's chorus, then appends two **new** lines: *"who are you without the mask? Did Hermes paint his face? I don't know, maybe."* A separate end-card reads *"Produced In Cooperation With B.MR.C"* (see [[BMRC]]).

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the recovered microsites (where the manifestos really live)
> Two of the cards turn out to be *abbreviations* of fuller texts that lived on now-dead Jagwar Twin microsites, recovered from web archives — both predating the [[The Hall of Mirrors|Hall of Mirrors]] ARG.
>
> **deathofmygeneration.fun** (the *Happy Face* "experiment," Dec 2020) carries the manifesto in a longer form than the card: *"We each have the power of the universe within us, and the power to change the world within us. But first we must each know and change ourselves. We are burning the old ways down to the ground, and that is a painful process, but out of the fire something new is being birthed. This is the death of my generation…"* It also carries the consume-satire copy — *"BUY! BUY! BUY!"* and *"WHAT TO WEAR! WHAT TO LIKE! HOW TO BE!"* — a facial-recognition *smile-gate* (*"Are you content?"*), and a phone gag, *"(313)-251-4932 / ARE YOU CONTENT? CALL NOW!"*
>
> **thechaosorder.academy** (the *Down to You* site, 2021) is the native home of that card's manifesto. The site's own meta-description is the card's seeker-line, *"What is it that you seek?"* It carries the concept-of-self manifesto verbatim and adds two lines the card omits: *"No one else can be IT, you must be IT for your own unique journey"* and *"This is the path you chose but there are others you could have taken."* The *"you must be IT"* phrasing firms the kingdom-within doctrine to a dated 2021 anchor and ties it to the Alan Watts *"you are it"* lineage that later resurfaces as *"you are the maze."*
>
> In Roy's 2022 culture3 interview he describes the *Happy Face* interactive directly: *"I wanted to experiment with how a fan could really immerse themselves psychologically and physiologically in a song,"* the smile-gate set against satirical consume-commands (Hollywood *"telling you what to wear and what to like and how to be"*) to *"get to the heart of the question the song asks."* He credits a free weekly guided-meditation space as the seed of the *Happy Face* project.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the "are you content?" easter egg had a back-end
> The phone number on the 2020 *Happy Face* site — *(313)-251-4932 / "ARE YOU CONTENT? CALL NOW"* — was independently cracked years later as a [[The Hall of Mirrors|Hall of Mirrors]] room code: the digits-only string `3132514932` unlocks the ARG's *"ARE YOU CONTENT?"* poster room. So the *"are you content?"* device runs across three layers of the project — the 2020 cards, the 2020 microsite, and the 2022 web3 ARG — with a working unlock buried in a years-old phone gag. The bit was load-bearing the whole time.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — Beth, the house, and the roof (the load-bearing 2026 card)
> The *Bounce* card is the one place the device prints an actual Hebrew letter, and three independent facts land on a single symbol. **Beth (ב) literally means "house."** The card epigraphs the *Sefer Yetzirah* passage on Beth. And *Bounce*'s own chorus is *"Let's tear the roof off of my house, my house."* The letter's meaning *is* the lyric's image — strong evidence the choice is deliberate, not decorative. Read together, the song reactivates Beth: the house turns from container into opening, closed to open, shelter to communion.
>
> Push one step further and the card's first line states the project's foundational tenet. Through Beth = house = heart, *"HE MADE THE LETTER BETH KING OVER WISDOM"* reads as *the heart enthroned as king over the mind* — the [[Heart and Mind|heart-over-mind]] doctrine, printed as scripture. The same verse's *"…He formed the Moon"* corroborates it (the Moon is the heart/lunar pole in the cosmology). This is a strong reading rather than a documented gloss — it routes through Beth = house = heart — but the intent reads as unmistakable.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — Hermes in the letter-slot
> On the *Welcome to the Circus* card, the small-print slot that held *Bounce*'s Hebrew letter now holds a Hermes riddle: *"Did Hermes paint his face? I don't know, maybe."* Hermes — the god who *invented letters and writing* — occupies the exact position where the previous card printed a letter. The move recasts the face-painting ringmaster ([[Sir Lucius]]) as Hermes / Thoth: trickster, inventor of writing, and psychopomp — the guide through the underworld, which lands pointedly given the *"Live **Underground**"* setting. He's the namesake of Hermeticism *and* hermeneutics (decoding itself), and alchemy's Mercurius, the duplex trickster — a tidy fit for a figure who *"tells the truth, but the truth deceives."* The shrugging *"I don't know, maybe"* performs the cipher's own open-question status; the card declines to confirm its own riddle, which is the joke and the honesty at once.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the "you are it" doctrine, and the Devil before the name
> Two doctrines printed on the cards deserve their lineage spelled out. **You-are-it / kingdom-within:** *"you're the Kingdom, you're the King"* (2021) = *"you are the maze"* (2026) = *"you must be IT"* (thechaosorder.academy, 2021) = Alan Watts's *"you are it"* = Chaplin's *"the kingdom of God is within man"* (sampled in [[Superhuman]]) / Luke 17:21. The self-is-sovereign axiom was on a public card in 2021. **The Devil:** *"Devil waking up to claim the kingdom"* (2021) puts the Devil/Lucius figure on a public card by function-name roughly two years before [[Sir Lucius]]'s 2023 named dream-origin — consistent with the project's principle that origins *accumulate*, they don't *begin*. The card is where the figure first becomes visible.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — "NONE," the Saturn-cake, and the No-Thing
> The *Down to You* card answers *"WHAT IS IT THAT YOU SEEK?"* with the sigil *"NONE"* — the no-thing of [[The Empty Mirror]], alongside the project's later *"no thing ever creates"* line. And the *I Like to Party* cover repays a close look: a cake plated as **Saturn / Cronus the devourer** — the god who eats his children — *plus cake* = [[Consumption]], ringed by skulls = *memento mori.* A birthday platter that eats you. Esoteric doctrine smuggled inside a dessert, which is the device's whole personality.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the named-influence map (and one open lead)
> The cards are a clean index of the project's stated and resonant sources. **Hermeticism / the Emerald Tablet** — *"as above, so below"* (2021), *"Did Hermes paint his face?"* (2026). **Kabbalah / Sefer Yetzirah** — the Beth passage on *Bounce* (likely via Aryeh Kaplan's translation); Beth = "house," the second letter (creation begins after Aleph's unity). **Alchemy** — Nigredo, the blackening (*Happy Face*); Mercurius the trickster-spirit (Hermes/Lucius). **Christian scripture** — Luke 17:21, the kingdom within. **Chaplin** — *The Great Dictator* speech, sampled in [[Superhuman]]. **Jung** — the persona = mask. **Alan Watts** — *"you are it."* **Roman myth** — Saturn / Cronus the devourer. **TV-infomercial advertising** — the *®*, *CALL NOW*, *PRESENTED BY* register, parodied throughout.
>
> One lead stays a lead, not a fact: the *Down to You* concept-of-self manifesto is near-verbatim **Neville Goddard** — *"change your conception of yourself and you change your world."* The thechaosorder.academy recovery firms it to a 2021 primary, but a Goddard attribution should be verified against a Goddard primary before it's stated as influence rather than resemblance.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the visual-canon thesis, and why the cipher question is open
> The strongest synthesis — guarded as synthesis, not fact — is that the Manifesto Cards are one of three recurring visual-canon devices (with the Realized Circus and the silent-Lucius end-tag) that argue the Jagwar Twin project is one continuous, pre-architected work: the 2026 *Lucius Lullaby* material is the *unveiling* of a mythology already fully present by 2020–2021, not its birth. See [[The Visual Canon Throughline]].
>
> A real caution rides alongside it. The cards' content genuinely *varies*, and not every release carries one — which is itself the argument against any rigid hidden cipher. The early wave (2019–2021) used **no** Hebrew letters; the 2026 wave introduced one (Beth) but the *Welcome to the Circus* card carried a Hermes riddle in the letter-slot instead of a letter. So a strict letter-by-letter code across the *Live Underground* cards is currently weakened, not confirmed. With only two 2026 cards in hand, whether they form a fresh coded series stays an open question — the decider is the next *Lucius Lullaby* card: another *Sefer Yetzirah* letter would point toward a selective letter-cipher; a lyrics-or-riddle card would leave only *"an esoteric epigraph per song, mixed traditions."* Best held, for now, as a recurring statement of values rather than a locked code.

## Source notes

- **Grounded (released videos, public posts, recovered public sites).** The cards themselves — the fixed template, the on-screen text, the sigils, the dates — are documented in dated, public @JagwarTwin videos. Verbatim quoted here: *Happy Face*'s *"are you content? / brought to you buy / death of my generation"* card on the inverted-face CRT; *I Like to Party*'s *"Devil waking up… you're the King"* end-card and Saturn-cake cover; *Down to You*'s *"what is it that you seek? / NONE / as above so below"* card; *Bounce*'s verbatim *Sefer Yetzirah* Beth epigraph; *Welcome to the Circus*'s *"who are you without the mask? / Did Hermes paint his face?"* card and the *"B.MR.C"* end-card. The two recovered microsites (deathofmygeneration.fun, thechaosorder.academy) and their fuller manifesto texts are public-archive fact. Roy's ~2020 *"are you content?"* diary line and his 2022 culture3 remarks on the *Happy Face* interactive are public. These are the load-bearing facts and they're public-facing.
- **Strong readings.** The doctrinal through-line (the cards carrying kingdom-within, the Devil, as-above-so-below, change-the-concept-of-self, and burn-it-down for years before the songs named them); Beth = house = heart and the heart-over-mind gloss; the Hermes-in-the-letter-slot recasting of Lucius; the *"are you content?"* triple pun; the Saturn-cake-as-Consumption read; the visual-canon throughline. Grounded in the material, but interpretive — held as readings, not claims.
- **Framework (esoteric, syncretic — never asserted as authorial intent).** The deeper letter-mysticism (Beth's planet/day/organ assignments, the Mercurius/Thoth layering, the psychopomp reading of *"Live Underground"*) is licensed by the cards' own openly Kabbalistic and Hermetic furniture, but the work never states it. You don't need any of it to read the page.
- **Open / unverified.** The Neville Goddard influence-lead needs a primary-source check. The *Welcome to the Circus* card's letter-slot riddle leaves the *Live Underground* cipher question genuinely open; the next card decides it. [[BMRC]]'s expansion remains uncracked.
