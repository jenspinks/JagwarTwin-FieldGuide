# The Realm

> **One-line reading.** The Realm is the parallel world Roy builds to route around the industry gatekeepers — the medieval counter-kingdom where the work reaches people once it's past the things that block it.

Where [[The Circus]] is the algorithmic gatekeeping system — the thing that blocks the work from getting through — the Realm is the counter-world built *outside* the gatekeepers' reach: renaissance-faire headlining, bardcore, the owned audience, blockchain releases. If the Circus is the blockade, the Realm is the route around it.

## Roy names it

"The realm" is Roy's own word, used repeatedly in the public community channels. Trying to release a song the gatekeepers were blocking, he wrote that they were keeping him from getting it *"to the realm"* — the realm being where the song goes once it's past the gate. He calls himself a *"loyal servant of the realm"* and speaks of *"guardians of the realm… here to keep the world safe,"* and frames its growth alongside the community: *"the realm will expand, the circle will grow."*

## A medieval aesthetic

The Realm has a consistent look: medieval, Renaissance, bardcore. Roy announced Jagwar Twin *"headlining the renaissance faire in LA,"* joked about a *"lute eras tour,"* and described an era where everyone would *"dress up in our future medieval gear"* — a "fantasy bardcore" world with its own costume and instrumentation. The aesthetic isn't decoration; it's the flag of the counter-kingdom.

## The route around the gatekeepers

The Realm reads as a parallel distribution world built outside label control. Its mechanisms each bypass a different gate: faire *headlining* is his own stage with no gatekeeper; *blockchain* releases sit where the label has no rights; the owned community is direct-to-fan, an audience no algorithm stands between. Where the Circus blocks and games attention, the Realm is the escape hatch — the world Roy builds when the gate won't open.

It carries the same double-bind as everything else in the project, though: the Realm still has to win attention to exist (the campaigns, the streams), so the escape is never perfectly clean. The route around the blockade still runs through the same weather.

## A note on the edges

The Realm and [[The Circle]] (the owned community) may be two names for one owned-world — Roy says *"the realm will expand, the circle will grow"* in a single breath. The relationship is left open here rather than collapsed. This page stays on Roy's public statements about the realm, the faire, and the blockchain releases.
