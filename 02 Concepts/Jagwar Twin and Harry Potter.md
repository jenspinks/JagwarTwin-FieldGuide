# Jagwar Twin and Harry Potter

> **One-line reading.** The one documented Harry Potter reference in Jagwar Twin is the song "dumbledore." Everything past that is convergence, not influence — both bodies of work drink from the same well of Western esoteric tradition, so the parallels are real but neither side is borrowing from the other.

The governing rule for this page: route every parallel through the shared *tradition*, never "Potter to Jagwar Twin" directly. To do otherwise would make a co-participant in a tradition look like a source — the same error that would wrongly turn any fellow-traveler into an origin. Sir Lucius and Lucius Malfoy share a light-name, and that's a resonance, not a derivation.

## The one documented anchor

**"dumbledore"** is a 2025 release named for the Potter character, with a "DARK ARTS VERSION" and wizard framing, and the public release saga carried the Dobby-freed-by-a-sock beat (*"Master gave dumbledore a sock… dumbledore is finally free"*). Roy framed it publicly as *"a love song,"* not a plot retelling. This is the named allusion — the one place a direct Potter reference is on the record. Everything below is not anchored by it; each parallel stands or falls on its own tradition-routing.

## Shared-source parallels — convergence, not influence

Both works show these motifs because both inherit the same traditions. The point of each pairing is the *explanation* in the last clause — that's the firewall.

- **Alchemy** (the Great Work; dissolution then rebirth; the red and white). In Potter it's a confirmed structuring device; in Jagwar Twin it's the [[Nigredo]] / blackening arc and transformation-through-dissolution. Shared tradition — not Potter-sourced.
- **The double and the shadow.** Potter has the unintegrated shadow and doubles across its houses; Jagwar Twin has [[Sir Lucius]], the mind split from the heart. Shared tradition (the Jungian shadow, the double).
- **The mirror.** Potter has the Mirror of Erised — desire against truth. Jagwar Twin has the source-versus-reflection problem, the Empty Mirror, the "magic mirror." Shared tradition (the mirror as a way of knowing).
- **Awakening and remembrance.** Both turn on hidden knowledge, a long game, remembering rather than acquiring. In Jagwar Twin this is already grounded canon: [[The Circle]], the "inside of the inside," remembrance not acquisition. Shared tradition (Gnostic and Platonic recollection).
- **Names and word-magic.** Potter's spells are Latinate words; true-names have power. Jagwar Twin reaches for the "magic of words," letters as creative force. Shared tradition (the word as creative power).
- **Death and rebirth.** Potter has the phoenix and the sacrificial mentor; Jagwar Twin has the Circle's recurrence and survival-through-rock-bottom. Shared tradition (the death-rebirth mystery).

**On the Lucius name.** [[Sir Lucius]] and Lucius Malfoy share the Latin light-field (*lux*; adjacent to Lucifer as light-bringer). In Potter this gives an aristocratic villain his irony; in Jagwar Twin it suits the brilliant intellect-shadow who brings a *dangerous* kind of light. This is an etymological resonance only — no derivation from Malfoy is documented, and Malfoy is in fact the weakest strand of the name. The stronger lineage runs through Lucifer and *lux*, and that's handled on the Sir Lucius page.

## Where the bridges stay speculative

A few Potter-specific bridges are attractive but should be held as resonance, not evidence: Sir Lucius and Lucius Malfoy (the etymology above); a "black magic rainbow circus" reading against Potter's alchemical colours (no artist-stated definition is on file); the Deathly Hallows geometry against any Jagwar Twin sigil (visual rhyme only — triangle, circle, line); Potter's sevenfold structure against the project's 33 (numbered symbolic systems, not numerological equivalence). None of these is a pillar.

## Why the page is shaped this way

Harry Potter is a co-participant in the Western esoteric tradition, not a source for Jagwar Twin. Alchemy isn't a "Potter parallel" just because Rowling also used alchemy. Keep the tradition, not the Potter frame, as the organizing axis. If a direct Roy reference to any specific Potter element beyond "dumbledore" ever surfaces — Malfoy, Hogwarts, the Horcruxes — that item graduates from resonance to documented. Until then, this map is convergence. The absence of any such reference is precisely *why* everything past "dumbledore" stays convergence.
