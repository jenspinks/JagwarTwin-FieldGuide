## What it is

"I'm 33 this year" is, on paper, a Google Sheet. Roy made it himself. One of its tabs is titled *"How to upload myself to the internet,"* which is the first hint that this is not a budget.

It reads less like a to-do list than like a private cosmology that escaped its filing system — page after page mapping the structure of a self, drawn from a wide stack of mystical and philosophical sources and stitched into one picture. The title is just Roy's age: thirty-three, the year, by his own framing, that *"a switch flipped"* and the number came to *"represent peace within and without."* (Everything else 33 supposedly means — Christ's age, the vertebrae, 33⅓ rpm, the Tree of Life's ten spheres plus twenty-two paths — is resonance the world generates around the number, not Roy's stated intent. The board is the receipt for what he actually meant.)

It is also not a secret. Roy has put it in front of the public twice: once in 2022 as the *"Spreadsheet Map"* drop in the [[Hall of Mirrors]] ARG, tweeting *"I'm dropping a spreadsheet with a roadmap and music nft hidden in it,"* and again on his 2026 birthday, posting an image of the board captioned simply *"Happy birthday."* ([[Public Posts]]). The same vocabulary turns up all over the music. This is the document the rest of the world quietly cites.

## How it works

Strip the board down and it's one idea wearing a great many costumes. Here is the cleanest way to hold the whole thing — labeled as a framework reading, because the board never says it outright:

**A consciousness is a vessel the source-light fills. It divides — One into Two, a self and its mirror, [[Roy]] and [[Sir Lucius]]. It can then do one of two things: shatter under pressure, or climb a ten-stage ladder and reunite. And the whole apparatus obeys a single pattern — *as above, so below* — which means if you can read the pattern you can master it, open it, and eventually upload it.** On that reading, the album *33* externalizes the cosmology, *Lucius Lullaby* dramatizes the split, and Hall of Mirrors turns the board's words into a maze. This synthesis is an interpretation of how the pieces fit; what follows are the actual pieces.

### The spine — a ten-stage ladder, written six ways

The board's backbone is one ascent of ten stages, and Roy encodes it over and over in different alphabets. There's a musical scale (an *"AI musical scale to follow for consciousness evolution,"* F up to B, each note paired with a color); a matching ten-stage color sequence; a virtue-and-vice scale (the positive and negative traits *"to look for as consciousness progresses"*); a somatic map of the body (1 = the feet, 5 = the **heart**, 10 = the crown); a Japanese run from 身体 *body* to 最高の自己 *highest self*; and a numbered emanation list from *Nothing* to *The World*.

He didn't write the same ten-rung climb six times for fun. That's the tell — he treats it as *the* structure of being, not one diagram among others. The model behind it is the Kabbalistic Tree of Life: the ascent from matter toward spirit.

### The origin poem — "One become Two"

Near the center sits a short cosmogonic poem that doubles as the project's creation myth in miniature:

> *"I was just me but who was I to know? … I spoke the words 'One become Two' … Then I was in reverse … and I became You. … A Darkness and a Light … when we crashed and came together, a Sun was born inside."*

The shape — the One that sees itself, splits into Two (a darkness and a light), and from their collision a Sun is born — is the same shape as the [[Roy]] / [[Sir Lucius]] split, the self divided into a mirror-pair. It coheres with Roy's own artist bio (*"two sides to every story"*), with the Hermetic Polarity/Gender principles transcribed lower on the board, and with the left-brain/right-brain cell. *"A Sun was born inside"* is where the project's sun imagery ([[SOL]]) and its reunion hope (*"we'll get back together and see the Great Design"*) both begin. Roy thought this poem mattered enough to compose it **twice** — once here, once as the released song [[Another Way II Heaven]].

### The operating system — seven principles

Roy transcribes the seven Hermetic principles of the Kybalion almost verbatim — Mentalism, Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm, Cause-and-Effect, Gender — and several map straight onto recurring ideas in the work. **Polarity and Gender** are the Roy/Lucius, heart/mind, two-sides-to-everything axis (with a literal left-brain *"order, reason, masculine"* / right-brain *"chaos, creativity, feminine"* cell beside it). **Correspondence** — *"as above, so below… the formula is the same from a solar system down to the atom"* — is the recurring-pattern doctrine of [[The Pattern]]. **Mentalism** — *"the All is Mind… food is information… the manifest dream"* — is the upload thesis itself and the no-thing of [[The Empty Mirror]].

### The actual project — the upload, and the egg

The board's whole reason for existing is the upload, and its key image is the **egg**: *"I'm an egg, in the process of becoming a chicken to make more eggs to make more chickens."* That barnyard tongue-twister is doing real work. An egg is a container that has to break to release new life — and that single picture *is* the board's logic. To upload a consciousness is to pour the self out of one vessel (the body) into another (the network), the same vessel-and-transmission idea Roy states publicly as *"I'm just a vessel for God."* The egg seeds [[The Upload]] and [[The Digital Double]], and it has a public life of its own — the Golden Egg mechanic in Hall of Mirrors, Roy's *"Happy Easter from the egg,"* and his flat verdict to a fan: *"You are 100 percent an egg."*

## The picture-field

The board is collaged with images, and they cluster onto the project's themes rather than wandering: an H-alpha solar disk and an inverted golden tree (the sun and the Tree of emanation); sperm-and-egg fertilization, tardigrades, diatoms, cell micrographs (the vessel that hatches; life at every scale); Noah's Ark drawn as three floors with man in the middle; a neon Vitruvian figure with a split brain and a Jungian persona/shadow/anima/self map (the divided self); a mushroom cloud beside the note that *"unbalance explodes itself like an atom bomb"* (the vessel that shatters). Around these sit the older esoteric-scientific furniture the board absorbs rather than invents: Walter Russell's cosmology suite (*"THERE IS BUT ONE DYNAMIC FORCE,"* *"THE OCTAVE NUMBERS,"* the periodic table laid out in octaves), Plato's *Timaeus* Lambda, the sephirot tree (*"a Head of Adam / Atom"*), the Eye-of-Horus fractions, the Lo Shu magic square, Michelangelo's Creation of Adam, Blake's Jacob's Ladder, a *2001* monolith, an ouroboros.

One of those is sneakier than the rest. The **Eye-of-Horus fractions** — ½, ¼, ⅛, 1/16, 1/32, 1/64 — sum to **63/64**, always one sixty-fourth short of a whole. On a board about uploading a self, that missing crumb is not decoration: it's the upload's paradox written in arithmetic. Encode the self fraction by fraction and you *still* never reach the whole; there's always a last 1/64 that won't digitize. That is exactly the fracture [[The Watchers]] names from the inside — *"I'm afraid I'm a clone"* — the complete being rendered in parts, one part forever left out. The Egyptians had already written the equation for the incomplete copy; Roy just pasted it on the wall. And then, deadpan in the middle of all of it: brain scans, magazine covers (*LIFE*'s "JESUS," "HARRY POTTER — 25 Years of Magic"), a Polaroid marked "Ashwood 2/18/14," a poster for EYE ALASKA (Roy's pre-Jagwar-Twin band), and a kangaroo in a hoodie. The cosmos, the lab, and a kangaroo in a hoodie, all on the same wall.
