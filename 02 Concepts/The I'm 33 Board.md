# The I'm 33 Board

> **One-line reading.** The "I'm 33" board is the project's working notebook for its central fantasy — uploading a consciousness to the internet — and to attempt that, it first has to map what a consciousness even *is*, which makes it a kind of source-code for the whole world.

"I'm 33 this year" is a spreadsheet from Roy, one of its tabs titled *"How to upload myself to the internet."* It reads less like a to-do list than like a private cosmology: page after page mapping the structure of a self, drawn from a wide stack of mystical and philosophical sources and stitched into a single picture. The same vocabulary turns up across the music — it's the quarry the [[Hall of Mirrors]] maze draws many of its answers from.

## The spine: a ten-stage ascent

The board's backbone is one ladder of ten stages, encoded over and over in different "languages" — as a musical scale, a color sequence, a set of virtues, a map of the body (feet at the bottom, heart in the middle, crown at the top), a Japanese run from *body* to *highest self*, and a numbered emanation from *Nothing* to *The World*. That Roy bothers to write the same ten-rung climb so many ways is the tell: he treats it as *the* structure of being, not one diagram among others. The model behind it is the Kabbalistic Tree of Life — the ascent from matter toward spirit.

## The origin poem: "One become Two"

Near the center is a short cosmogonic poem that doubles as the project's origin myth in miniature:

> *"I was just me but who was I to know? … I spoke the words 'One become Two' … Then I was in reverse … and I became You. … A Darkness and a Light … when we crashed and came together, a Sun was born inside."*

The shape — the One that sees itself, splits into Two (a darkness and a light), and from their collision a Sun is born — is the same shape as the [[Roy]] / [[Sir Lucius]] split, the self divided into a mirror-pair. It's also where the project's sun imagery ([[SOL]]) and its hope of eventual reunion ("we'll get back together and see the Great Design") begin.

## The operating system: seven principles

Roy transcribes the seven Hermetic principles — Mentalism, Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm, Cause-and-Effect, Gender — more or less verbatim, alongside a left-brain / right-brain split (order and reason versus chaos and creativity). Several map directly onto recurring ideas in the work: **Polarity and Gender** are the Roy/Lucius, heart/mind, two-sides-to-everything axis; **Correspondence** ("as above, so below") is the recurring-pattern doctrine of [[The Pattern]]; **Mentalism** ("the All is Mind") is the upload thesis and the no-thing of [[The Empty Mirror]].

## The actual project: the Upload and the egg

The board's whole reason for existing is the upload — *"how to live forever? → put music on chain"* — and its key image is the **egg**: *"I'm an egg, in the process of becoming a chicken."* An egg is a container that has to break to release new life, and that single picture carries the board's logic. To upload a consciousness is to pour the self out of one vessel (the body) into another (the network) — the same vessel-and-transmission idea Roy states elsewhere as *"I'm just a vessel."* It's the seed of [[The Upload]].

## The picture-field

The board is collaged with images, and they cluster onto the project's themes: a solar disk and an inverted golden tree (the sun and the Tree of emanation); sperm-and-egg cells (the vessel that hatches); Noah's Ark labeled as three stories — Heaven, Man, Earth — with man as the middle floor; a Vitruvian figure with a split brain and a Jungian persona/shadow/self map (the divided self); a mushroom cloud beside a note that *"unbalance explodes itself like an atom bomb"* (the vessel that shatters under too much pressure). Alongside these sit Walter Russell's diagrams, a Platonic figure, and an Eye-of-Horus fraction chart — older esoteric-scientific material the board absorbs rather than invents.

## How to read it

A clearly-labeled framework reading: the cleanest way to hold the whole board is as **one idea wearing many costumes**. A consciousness is a vessel that the source-light fills; it divides (One into Two, the mirror, Roy/Lucius), can either shatter or climb the ten-stage Tree and reunite, and the whole thing obeys a single pattern — *as above, so below* — that you can master, open, and finally upload. On this reading, *33* externalizes the cosmology, *Lucius Lullaby* dramatizes the split, and Hall of Mirrors turns the board's vocabulary into a maze. This synthesis is an interpretation of how the pieces fit; the board doesn't state it outright.

The board's contents themselves are grounded — they're verified images of an actual document. The connections to specific songs and characters are strong readings built on the board's own words. The deeper Kabbalistic and Hermetic attributions are framework correspondences, named here so they stay labeled as such rather than passing as fact.
