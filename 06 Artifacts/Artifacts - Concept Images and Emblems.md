"Logos" (divine word/reason) glossed as "Ratio" restates the Greek double meaning of logos as both word and proportion, so the diagram equates cosmic order with a load-bearing structure held in balance, a Pattern-cosmology page consistent with Roy's other Hall of Mirrors cosmological sketches (the "Undefined Map," the Upload recipe).
