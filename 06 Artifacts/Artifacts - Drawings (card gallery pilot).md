### 5 · Jupiter the Blue Dolphin
An upright dolphin-creature with a large eye, pentagon-soled shoes, a hand-lettered **"G"** on its chest, and an alchemical triangle-crown floating above, captioned *"Jupiter the Blue Dolphin."* **Jupiter** (Chesed/mercy, the expansive benefic and classical sky-father) embodied as the dolphin — the soul-animal that crosses the waters. The code **`LADDER`** makes Jupiter the *rung of ascent*: Jacob's Ladder and the "I'm 33" board's Tree-of-Life emanation ladder ("0. Nothing" → "10. The World"). The "G" (God / the Great Architect's letter) crowns it a divine node.
**Connects** [[City of Angels]] · [[The Pattern]] · [[Kabbalah - The Tree of Life]] — **[room ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/rooms/0x86154a585c03e88a49b4f8a91b5119b60356f5aee3203585979a1c26d6cad56e)** · **[gallery ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/gallery)**

### 8 · Walking the Edge of a Knife
A hidden Roy poem (never a released song) typeset over a medieval **millefleurs tapestry** (flowering foliage with rabbits, the Unicorn-Tapestry idiom). The verse runs a knife-edge between *"I wana live / I wana die,"* addresses a feminine "you" three ways (*"my mistress / my wife / my witness"*), and closes *"Used to be simple… Now it's all symbols."* The code **`INSIDE OF THE INSIDE`** is verbatim **The Circle**'s central refrain; the poem's *"turn back the stone / From the inside"* re-images that as a resurrection rolled back from within.
**Connects** [[The Circle]] · [[The Thread]] · [[Mary]] — **[room ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/rooms/0xe858c547827aba7438709a166b469d26170394ba55ffbf865b0ebecea03f3c6a)** · **[gallery ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/gallery)**

### 9 · Roman Calculus
A **Roman abacus / counting-board** — vertical columns crossed by two bars, beads in upper and lower registers. Arithmetic as a physical, positional act. The code **`9360`** is a round, calendar-flavoured figure (360 × 26); the artifact *is a number rendered as a diagram of how numbers are made.* It sits with the cosmology-diagrams that treat creation as counting and emanation, but on a quieter signal than the keystones.
**Connects** [[The Pattern]] · [[Kabbalah - The Tree of Life]] — **[room ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/rooms/0xe0e13d94ee1e14ef20ee89e165997c7e4ce15965c767c5b8079ac13ace454df4)** · **[gallery ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/gallery)**

### 15 · Tree Thinking
A pyramid of ten strata (1→10, apex to base), *"Abyss In"* at the top of the descent, *"Abyss Out"* near the base, a keystone at the bottom. The **Tree of Life as a graded pyramid**: the ten levels are the Sefirot, the "Abyss" the chasm severing the supernal triad (Keter-Chokmah-Binah) from the seven lower rungs, crossed only through hidden **Da'at**. One of the "I'm 33" board's own diagrams, minted one at a time.
**Connects** [[Kabbalah - The Tree of Life]] · [[The Pattern]] · [[Another Way II Heaven]] — **[room ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/rooms/0x8a9fe05bb3dbf271b4ab76f457b52a14dda44f3eb42edc5f4da3743febbe4bd9)** · **[gallery ↗](https://mirrors.jagwartwin.com/gallery)**

### 17 · 2014 Self Portrait
A notebook self-portrait labelled **"ME"** with a small **cross-mark on the forehead** (third-eye), a floating cross at left, a **snake weeping from the right eye**, and *"VALE(N)T DAY"* at lower right. The code **`DON'T BE A BLACK HOLE`** is a self-injunction — a black hole is a collapsed star that *devours* light instead of radiating it, the exact inverse of *"your soul is a Star."* The forehead cross is the same third-eye mark Roy wears as **Sir Lucius**'s face-paint — a documented pre-Lucius self-image, the shadow's iconography latent on his own face nearly a decade before the character is named.

### 26 · Suggestion of Apex
