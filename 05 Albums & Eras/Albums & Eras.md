# Albums & Eras

The larger arcs of the Jagwar Twin project, in order — the soil, the cosmology, the connective tissue between, and the descent.

## The eras, in order

- [[Subject to Flooding]] — the 2018 debut. A record made in the wilderness years that diagnoses the disease the rest of the catalogue spends years treating. Its *we-not-me* humanism already contains the seed of everything after: the protective survival-self, the borrowed older voices, the first naming of the Circle.

- [[33]] — the 2022 cosmology album. The personal wound of the debut turns outward into a diagnosis of the attention age; it poses the catalogue's central question at its literal center and answers it with the founding myth at the close — all before any of it is named "Lucius."

- [[Singles & EPs]] — the standalone releases between the albums. More than once the most important developments happened off-album: the dream that births Sir Lucius, and the 2023–2025 run of singles and EPs that quietly assemble the figure the 2026 album finally names.

- [[Lucius Lullaby (album)]] — the 2026 album. A sustained descent held almost to the end, where the shadow gets the microphone and the only thing that finally resolves is the survival of the feeling underneath. The first time the project shares its billing with the voice it was built to contain.
