# The Moon

> **One-line reading.** The moon is the project's image of borrowed light — a body that shines only because something brighter is shining on it, and whose whole meaning turns on whether it remembers that.

## At a glance

- **What it does** — names **reflected light**: a body that generates nothing of its own and shines only because the sun shines on it. The work uses it to ask its central question — does a reflected thing point back to what lit it, or set up as a light in its own right?
- **Carried by** — [[Roy]] (occupies the lunar *position* — the vessel emptied to receive) · [[Mary]] (carries the moon's *theology* — reflected light in exile, promised restoration) · [[Sir Lucius]] works *through* the moon's reflective realm without being its proper occupant (he's better placed as the morning star).
- **Appears in** — [[Ballerina Boy]] · [[Bad Feeling (Oompa Loompa)]] · [[#1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]] · [[MAGIK]] · *The Song of the Sun & the Moon (33 Demo)* · the Roy X moon-aphorisms · the *Soul iOS* full-moon post · the [[Sir Rabbit]] poem · the [[The Pattern|"Undefined Map"]] correspondence sketch · the visual canon.
- **Confidence** — grounded fact (verbatim moon lines, dated public posts, an actual released moon-titled track) braided with strong interpretation and an openly framework-tier esoteric reading.

## What it does

The moon is the catalogue's figure for **reflected light**. It makes nothing of its own; everything it gives off is the sun's light, bounced. That single fact *is* the operation. The moon is light that is not its own source — and the whole drama is whether a given moon-figure remembers that or forgets it.

What's worth noticing is that the *same* image does different work in different songs. The moon is not one fixed character; it's a **position** several figures can occupy, and the charge shifts with who's standing in it:

- **Light reflected faithfully** — the vessel that knows it's a vessel ([[Roy]]).
- **Light that has dimmed but is promised back** — the exiled feminine awaiting restoration ([[Mary]]).
- **Light claiming to be the source** — reflection that forgets it's reflected (the lunar danger, the [[Sir Lucius]] register).
- **Light that holds steady through the dark** — preservation when the night is all there is (the *Ballerina Boy* pole).

The image is constant; the meaning is set by the song. And that keeps the moon tied to the larger frame the whole project runs on. A reflection is not false because it is a reflection. **It becomes false only when it forgets it is reflected** — which is the exact line the [[The Mirror|mirror]] turns on, and why the moon and the mirror are two faces of one idea. The moon is a special case of [[Source vs Reflection]]: sun versus reflected body, source versus the light that either returns to it or replaces it.

A standing guardrail, because the symbol is easy to flatten: the moon is reflected, borrowed, even *artificial* light — and it **nevertheless preserves hope through the dark.** That doubleness is the point. Like Lucius, the moon holds distortion-and-survival, artifice-and-preservation in one body. This is not "technology bad" and it is not "moon = fake." *Preservation without origin* is a real thing the symbol does, and it's tender.

## Associations

**[[Roy]] — the one who occupies the lunar position.** In the project's deep frame the moon is *Roy's*: the vessel that has nothing of its own and receives entirely from the source, the self who operates "from the heart" and can say *"I have become nothing."* The moon's emptiness, on this reading, isn't failure — it's the right relation to the light. (Roy is also, literally, a **Leo Moon** — see below — and his recurring move is the moon *reaching toward* the sun, not being it.)

**[[Mary]] — the moon's theology, not its position.** She is the diminished, face-hidden presence who reflects a light not her own and is promised restoration. The grounded anchor is in [[#1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]]: *"Saw a halo but I didn't see a head"* — the source glimpsed, not yet possessed; the crown reached but unpossessable. Roy holds the moon's position; Mary holds its **personhood and ascent**.

**[[Sir Lucius]] — the reflective register, but not the moon itself.** In the clean public read he sits at the *edge* of the moon's danger: reflected light under distortion, lit by fear and shame and reflecting fear and shame back, behaving as though the borrowed light were his own. In the project's corrected map, though, he's more precisely the **morning star** (Venus, the herald routinely mistaken for the dawn it only announces); the moon's reflective image-realm is his *operating theater*, not his home. Both readings agree on the danger — a light mistaking itself for the source. They disagree on the address. (See the Advanced reading on the lunar danger.)

## Appears in

**[[Ballerina Boy]]** *(standalone single, 2025)* — the **tender pole.** The track closes on a spoken outro in an older, motherly voice — a [[Mary]]-class blessing — that lands the symbol whole: *"look up to the sky / the moon reminds us that even in the darkness there is light."* The moon as proof that light survives the night even when the night is all you can see. This may be the most load-bearing symbolic line in the catalogue: the moon *reflects rather than generates* (the lunar signature) and it *preserves hope through darkness* (the tender pole).

**[[Bad Feeling (Oompa Loompa)]]** *(Lucius Lullaby, track 5; originally a 2023 single)* — the **sick pole.** *"When the moon hits the sky, I'm a little unwell."* Where Ballerina Boy preserves hope under the moon, here the dread *arrives* under borrowed light: luna → lunacy, moon-madness. Roy used the line as the album's promo headline (*"the truth is a lie that nobody can tell,"* over a *"when the moon hit the sky"* overlay). The two poles — *even in the darkness there is light* versus *unwell under the moon* — are the same image's tender and sick faces.

**[[#1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]]** *(Lucius Lullaby, track 8)* — the moon's **theology**, named. Mary arrives faceless — *"Mary, Mary, so contrary… saw a halo but I didn't see a head… La Conquistadora with a gun in her hand… fawning over no man"* — the lunar-feminine presence here, but her source still hidden. The "halo but no head" is the grounded faceless-divine-feminine anchor the moon's theology turns on.

**[[MAGIK]]** *(LOOP EP, 2025)* — *"a lasso 'round the Moon."* Reaching for the unreachable as a love-gesture; the moon as the impossible object you'd rope from the sky to give someone. (On the resonance — *It's a Wonderful Life*, where George Bailey offers to "lasso the moon" for a woman named **Mary** — see the Advanced reading; it's a soft echo, not an asserted reference.)

***The Song of the Sun & the Moon (33 Demo)*** — an actual, *released* Jagwar Twin moon track that almost nobody has heard. It exists only as a **Sound.xyz NFT** — his first Sound drop, around October 2022, edition of 25, sold out in under a minute — and lives **nowhere** on streaming. Roy's artist note is the key text: he wrote it alone in the woods in Ithaca, NY, across from the Dalai Lama's Namgyal Monastery, watching the sunset with an acoustic guitar, freshly dropped from a major label — *"a lullaby to myself, from myself. It reminds me that every night I die, and every morning I am reborn."* That death-and-rebirth gloss is Roy's own, stated: the sun sets, the moon rises, the self dies nightly and is reborn at dawn. (Whether this 2022 demo is the seed of [[SOL]], the wordless closer of *Lucius Lullaby*, is an open question — see the Advanced reading.)

**The Roy X aphorisms** *(2023, dated public posts)* — the moon as a recurring *personal* motif, not just a song image. A cluster runs across the year: *"The moon is a flashing woman"* (Jan 29) · *"The moon is a computer"* (May 30) · *"Notice the moon / becoming the sun"* (May 5) · *"Greetings from the other side of the moon"* (May 11) · and the deadpan sign-off *"Have a great Moon Day"* (Feb 28). *"Notice the moon / becoming the sun"* is Roy stating the lunar→solar arc in his own words: reflected light becoming source.

**The *Soul iOS* post** *(Modern Archeology series, 2021)* — Roy's earliest dated moon-plus-interiority fusion: a **full-moon** image overlaid *"All the answers are within you,"* captioned by a collaborator *"soul iOS."* Soul-as-software, moon in the reflection register — predating the whole metaverse/oracle era.

**The visual canon** — the moon recurs as literal **stage lighting**, which is exactly on the nose for reflected, artificial light. In the theatrical *Interview* series, **moon-like set-lights** hang over each of Lucius's shoulders (beside Tree-of-Life pillars, a fruit-bowl altar, an Egyptian statue). That fruit-bowl altar sitting *under* the moon-set-lights is not incidental: the [[The Banana|banana]] is the project's broken-Yesod image — generativity replaced by mere image — and Yesod is the moon's own sphere, the place reflected light belongs. The altar and the moon-light share one address. The 2023-era *Bad Feeling* video stages the moon inside the realized [[The Circus|Circus]]. And the 2026 *Bad Feeling* visualizer (co-credited "Jagwar Twin, sir lucius") seats the now-voiced Lucius on a **crescent-moon lullaby stage** with cotton clouds and a gold star balloon — the menace played as cartoonish nursery décor.

**The [[Sir Rabbit]] poem** *(Hall of Mirrors, Room 51, GENESIS)* — the strangest and most important moon-appearance, because it's the only place the moon is shown as **a machine.** *"The moon was violet in the sky"*; the Cat promises *"I'll take you to the moon for lunch"*; and on arrival — *"They came upon The Violet Moon but it was a machine / with whirling gears and funny ears that always kept it clean."* The reflector revealed as apparatus. (Roy called this poem *"sir rabbit pre-lucius."*) See the Advanced reading on the machine-moon.

**The "Undefined Map" sketch** *(Hall of Mirrors artifact)* — the moon as a **correspondence**, in Roy's own hand. The map is a freehand cosmology drawing that arranges planetary correspondences in columns: under one planet Roy writes **"sun + moon"** *and* **"both girl + boy"** together — the lunar and solar, the feminine and masculine, held as a single fused pole rather than opposed — and under the 7-Sisters / Labyrinth column he notes **"moonless nights."** This is the most direct evidence that the moon is, for Roy, a node in a deliberate [[The Pattern|As-Above-So-Below correspondence system]] — that the moon-imagery across the songs isn't loose poetry but plotted against a personal map where celestial bodies, genders, and states line up. "Sun + moon / both girl + boy" under one heading is the doubleness guardrail drawn out as cosmology; "moonless nights" names the lunar register's *absence* — the dark with no reflected light at all. See the Advanced reading on the correspondence map.

**Lunar timing, in Roy's own hand** — the moon also runs as a *scheduling* logic across his public Discord: *"8/11/22 33 full sturgeon moon ai day"*; *"intuitive that tonight is a full moon for Lucius and his voice sounds like the wind"* (2023); the breezy 2025 asides *"love a moon session"* and *"full moon in libra tomorrow."* The album-AI-and-character work is timed to actual moons.

**Two more real-world moon footprints.** Roy curates **Manly P. Hall**'s video *"Race for the Moon"* in his Discord "influences" channel — a grounded, Roy-posted esoteric thread with *moon* in the title. And the project literally **went to a moon**: Roy posted that *"Happy Face"* was sent toward **Europa**, Jupiter's moon, aboard NASA's Europa Clipper via the "Message in a Bottle" program. A Jagwar Twin song is, at this writing, en route to a moon.

## Related traditions

The moon's roots are named, and several are licensed by Roy's own documented engagement rather than imposed from outside.

- **Kabbalah / Hermetic Qabalah** — the moon as the lunar vessel on the [[Kabbalah - The Tree of Life|Tree of Life]] that *"receives entirely"* from the sun; the diminished-moon myth and its restoration arc (detailed below). Held framework-tier, never as authorial intent — but Roy's Kabbalah engagement is public (the *I'm 33* board, the *El Roi* tattoo).
- **Sefer Yetzirah** — Roy reproduced its **Beth passage** verbatim on a public manifesto card: *"He made the letter Beth king over wisdom… and with them He formed the Moon in the universe, Sunday in the year, and the right eye in the soul, male and female."* The card itself says *Moon.* (Reads as Aryeh Kaplan's translation.) This ties the moon to the heart-letter — Beth = house = heart.
- **Marian theology** — the Assumption and Coronation of the Virgin (the Daughter, become the Bride, enthroned as the Mother); *La Conquistadora* / Queen of Heaven, repurposed in [[#1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]].
- **Hermetic / Golden-Dawn Tarot** — the light-finale **Star → Moon → Sun** (XVII reflection's herald → XVIII reflection/illusion, the last deception → XIX true light). Kept stream-separate from the Jewish-Lurianic material.
- **Astrology** — moon-signs as a reading tool. Roy is a Gemini Sun, **Leo Moon**, Libra Rising (stated on IG Live), and reads listeners' natal moons in his Mirror Sessions (telling one guest *"your moon is Leo"*); he also times work to lunar events.
- **Theosophy / Western esoteric synthesis** — **Manly P. Hall** (*The Secret Teachings of All Ages*; the *"Race for the Moon"* video) as a Roy-posted influence and proximate popularizer of the whole toolkit.
- **Lunar etymology — luna → lunacy** — moon-madness, "unwell under the moon" ([[Bad Feeling (Oompa Loompa)]]); a corrupted echo of Dean Martin's moon-romance ("That's Amore").
- **Neoplatonism / Plato's Cave** — reflections and shadows versus the source, recoverable by turning back toward the light — the moon as the philosophically *near* light you can still navigate by until dawn.
- **Egyptian** — the Eye of Ra / Horus as "sun & moon eyes," feeding the watching-network and [[The Upload]] reading.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the Kabbalistic spine (the moon as Roy's position; the diminished-moon arc)
> In the project's syncretic Kabbalah, the Moon maps to **Malkhut + Yesod** — the only sefirot either tradition calls lunar — the lunar vessel that *"has nothing of its own, but receives entirely from Tiferet"* (the Sun). On that reading the lunar position is **Roy's**: the self emptied to receive.
>
> The arc the symbol keeps drawing is the **diminished-moon aggadah** (Talmud, *Chullin* 60b): the moon was once the sun's equal, was made to diminish, and God seeks atonement for diminishing her. Its restoration proof-text is **Isaiah 30:26** — *"the light of the moon shall become as the light of the sun."* Folded with **Cordovero's *atarah*** (Malkhut *becomes* the crown the moment she ascends), this is the literal engine under *"reclaim your crown."* "The moon trying to become a sun" is therefore **Roy's** arc — diminishment → restoration → crowning — not a villain's overreach. The theology of the exile-and-return belongs to **Mary** as the **Shekhinah** / *Galut ha-Shekhinah* (the divine feminine in exile), and her ascent runs **Malkhut → Binah** (the Assumption / Coronation). *Halo but no head* = the crown reached at the top of that ascent, present but unpossessable. **Framework-tier, syncretic — never claimed as Roy's intent.**

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the lunar danger, and why Lucius is the morning star, not the moon
> The clean public read holds [[Sir Lucius]] in the moon's reflective register — reflected light under distortion, mistaking itself for the sun. An older framing went further and called him *"the moon trying to become a sun… the tragedy, and the reason he reads as morally complex rather than evil"* — reflected light lit by fear and shame, reflecting them back. That clean read still works as a description of his *lunar danger*, and it lingers in some song essays.
>
> But the corrected map (2026) re-assigns the addresses. The moon-as-*position* is **Roy's** (Malkhut + Yesod); "the moon trying to become a sun" is **Roy's** restoration arc. **Lucius is the morning star** — Venus, *Lucifer* being Latin for the morning star — the herald that rises just before the sun, outshines all but sun and moon, and is routinely mistaken for the dawn it only announces. His native placement is Netzach; **Yesod (the reflective image-realm) is his operating *theater*, not his home.** He works *through* the moon's reflection without ever being its proper occupant. The two readings agree on the danger (a borrowed light claiming to be the source) and differ only on whose light it is. See [[The Light-Bearer]].

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the machine-moon (the Sir Rabbit poem)
> In the [[Sir Rabbit]] poem, the moon — normally *the* reflector — is revealed as **apparatus**: *"The Violet Moon… was a machine / with whirling gears and funny ears that always kept it clean."* Reflection become *mechanism.* It's staged as **charm-then-reveal**: the beautiful violet sky-moon comes *first*, the clockwork contraption *second* — beauty conceals the apparatus, the exact Lucius dynamic where helpfulness precedes the felt manipulation. Read forward, this is the moon→machine→Algorithm pipeline: severed reflection doesn't just trap, it *industrializes* into the [[The Circus|Circus]] / the attention economy (Yesod, the moon-sphere, mechanized into an attention market). It connects to the *"the moon is a computer"* aphorism and the *Soul iOS* full-moon-as-soul-software fusion — the moon feeding the soul-machine reading of [[The Digital Double]] and [[The Upload]]. Crucially, the doubleness still holds: the machine is also the thing that *"always kept it clean"* — preservation, even here.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the "Undefined Map" as As-Above-So-Below method
> The Hall of Mirrors **"Undefined Map"** is a freehand cosmology sketch — Roy's own correspondence chart, the [[The Pattern]] made literal. It places the moon inside a Hermetic **As-Above-So-Below** grid where celestial bodies, genders, and conditions are aligned in columns. Two entries matter for the moon. First, under a single planet Roy writes **"sun + moon"** *together with* **"both girl + boy"** — the lunar/solar and feminine/masculine pairs fused under one heading rather than split across two. That is the page's doubleness guardrail drawn as cosmology: the moon is not the simple opposite of the sun but co-located with it, the way [[Duality]] keeps the heart/mind poles inside one body. Second, under the **7-Sisters / Labyrinth** (Pleiades) column he notes **"moonless nights"** — the lunar register at its zero point, the dark with *no* reflected light, the condition the *Ballerina Boy* outro answers (*"even in the darkness there is light"*). The map is grounded — an artifact in Roy's hand — but the *system* it implies (which planet governs which sefira, which gender) is read as Roy's private syncretic scheme, not a public doctrine. It is the strongest single proof that the moon-imagery is **plotted**, not improvised. **Treat the map as grounded; the correspondence theology it sketches as framework-tier.**

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the Star → Moon → Sun finale, and the SOL question
> Stream-disciplined as Hermetic / Golden-Dawn (kept apart from the Jewish-Lurianic material above): the Major Arcana light-finale runs **Star (XVII)** — the morning-star herald (Lucius) — into **Moon (XVIII)** — reflection and illusion, the *last deception* before the truth — into **Sun (XIX)**, true light. Reflected/false light resolving into true light is the same operation as the Kabbalistic spine; both converge on the Sun. That Sun is plausibly **[[SOL]]** (*sol* = sun; *S*ong *O*f *L*ove), the wordless closer Lucius can't metabolize — and *The Song of the Sun & the Moon (33 Demo)* (2022) may well be SOL's origin: a worded demo whose finished 2026 form is words-*stripped*, the two ends of one song; *"I don't know if it will ever be finished"* matching SOL's unfinished feel; *"a lullaby to myself"* matching the album *Lucius Lullaby.* Held open — a strong case awaiting an ear-check against the still-downloadable demo audio.

> [!note]- Advanced reading — the lunar/Yesodic register of the early work
> [[Long Time Coming]] (*Subject to Flooding*, 2018) runs in a purely **lunar** key: mirrors, reflection, recursion, moonlight, identity-mediation — awareness without integration, orbit without return. The frame: *the moon is still necessary because dawn has not yet arrived.* It's the catalogue's clearest portrait of the lunar condition *before* the solar one is available — the self circling its own reflection, lit but not yet integrated. (A lighter open thread: "Phoebe" in [[Good Day]] may carry lunar resonance — Phoebe the Titan, "bright moon," grandmother of Artemis — given the album's Yesodic register. Under-anchored; noted, not load-bearing.)

- **Grounded** *(released music, dated public posts, public artifacts).* The verbatim moon lines — Ballerina Boy's outro, *"when the moon hits the sky, I'm a little unwell"* ([[Bad Feeling (Oompa Loompa)]]), *"a lasso 'round the Moon"* ([[MAGIK]]), *"saw a halo but I didn't see a head"* ([[#1 Champion (la Conquistadora)]]). - **Strong readings.** The moon-as-position (faithful / dimmed / claiming / steadfast); the tender-pole vs sick-pole split; the moon-and-mirror as one idea; the machine-moon's "beauty conceals the apparatus" and the moon→Algorithm pipeline; Long Time Coming's lunar register. Built on grounded anchors, labeled as interpretation.
- **Framework** *(esoteric, syncretic — never authorial intent).* The Kabbalistic spine (Moon = Roy's Malkhut+Yesod position; the diminished-moon → Isaiah 30:26 → *atarah* restoration arc; Mary as Shekhinah, Malkhut→Binah); Lucius re-placed as the morning star / Netzach; the Star→Moon→Sun tarot finale; the correspondence *system* implied by the "Undefined Map" (which planet governs which sefira/gender) and the [[The Banana|banana]]-as-broken-Yesod link sharing the moon's sphere. Licensed by Roy's open Kabbalah and esoteric engagement; the work never states them outright.
- **Open questions.** Is *The Song of the Sun & the Moon (33 Demo)* the origin of [[SOL]]? Is the *Bad Feeling* "unwell under the moon" a deliberate inversion of "That's Amore"? Is Mary's facelessness Roy's limit (he can't see her yet) or her own choice? Does the catalogue depict the *ascent* up the Tree (the moon restored) or only the break and its repair? Held open.
