# Characters

The recurring figures of the Jagwar Twin world — most of them facets of one self, defined by how they relate to each other.

## The cast

- [[Roy]] — the heart; the vulnerable, authentic self the whole project is trying to return to.
- [[Sir Lucius]] — the mind divorced from the heart; the protector that risks becoming a prison, and the figure the 2026 album is named for.
- [[Mary]] — the divine-feminine counter-voice; the faceless blessing-presence that says *you were born to shine.*
- [[Sir Rabbit]] — the seeker before the mask; Roy's image of himself as still-open and searching, before any guide hardens.
- [[Jagwar Twin]] — the project itself; not a character but the stage all the voices share.
